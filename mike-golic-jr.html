It engages with fans while discussing topics of the day and looking ahead at the night’s events. Mike joined the EPSN network in November 2015, as a host of a college football and NFL-focused podcast.

Additionally, Mike is a regular fixture on ESPN’s social media and digital content surrounding marquee events and key announcement shows such as the Super Bowl, NFL Draft, and College Football Playoff Rankings Reaction. He has also served as a guest host on the Mike & Mike show, a college football analyst show on ESPN Radio, during the broadcast of the 2015 Hawaii Bowl.
