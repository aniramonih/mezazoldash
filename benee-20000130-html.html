Stella Rose Bennett, better known as Benee and formerly Bene, is a New Zealand singer and songwriter from Auckland. In 2019 and 2020, she consecutively won Single of the Year, Best Solo Artist and Best Pop Artist at the New Zealand Music Awards. Benee first gained local popularity with the singles "Glitter" and "Soaked", before her 2019 single "Supalonely" gained international popularity following its success on the video-sharing platform TikTok. Benee then released her debut album Hey UX in November 2020. Under the nickname Bene, she made her solo debut with the 2017 single "Tough Guy", before performing with her 2018 single "Soaked"., A been certified double platinum in New Zealand. She released her debut EP, Fire on Marzz, with help from producer Josh Fountain in June 2019
