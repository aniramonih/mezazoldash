Who Is Gijs Van Der Most And How Did The Couple Meet?

Dutch photographer Gijs van der Most, born September 16, has worked for various renowned media sites. He studied photography at the Utrecht School for Arts (HKU). Gijs works alone and rarely posts about his personal life on social media.

Long-distance dating began for the pair in the late 2000s. After two years, they decided to escalate the LDR. They married in 2010 after considering a lifetime together. The couple married on July 31, 2010. Gijs has spent his career in Amsterdam. He and his wife now live in Brooklyn, New York.

The photographer’s website lists Esquire, People, Men’s Health, LPV Magazine, Fast Company, and others as clients. He says his photos are “driven from a genuine interest in people and their environment.”

Buteau And Gijs Share Two Kids Together

The couple has been happy together since marriage, but having kids has been challenging. They tried to have children but were not able to do so. In an essay for Glamour, the comedian said she had been told she had Prolactinoma.

This means that she had high amounts of a hormone that affects breast milk and can make it hard to get pregnant. They tried for another year. During that time, Buteau underwent a few rounds of IVF and lost four pregnancies.

The couple tried to sell their house to pay for a surrogate, but her mother stepped in to help them financially. They finally found a surrogate who could give birth to their twins Hazel and Otis van der Most. The couple doesn’t post many pictures of their kids on social media; when they do, the kids’ faces are always covered.
