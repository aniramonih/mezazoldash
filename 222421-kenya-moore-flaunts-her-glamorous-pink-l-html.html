Kenya Moore Flaunts Her Glamorous Pink Look While Riding a Bentley (Photo)

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 11, 2024

'Real Housewives of Atlanta' star Kenya Moore looked glamourous as she showed off flawless makeup while dressed in a white top and sitting in a Bentley.

As "Real Housewives of Atlanta" resumed filming, Kenya Moore confirmed her return to the show's 12th season. She proudly announced her continuation on the show by asking fans to apply for the position of her assistant.

In her caption, she referred to the film, "Devils Wears Prada" and teased her fans, saying "Miranda Priestly needs a new assistant." Although it is not known if the post was a joke, Moore laid out strict guidelines for potential applicants.

While juggling reality television, her business, and motherhood, Moore has a lot on her plate. The ageless beauty has managed to juggle her busy life. During a rare confession about her personal life, she showed off a few gray hairs. While proudly flaunting them, Moore said:

"You gave me these gray hairs and it was worth it Have a blessed day."

Moore is a strong, proud advocate of natural hair. The reality star has a haircare line called "Kenya Moore Haircare," which sells products that cater to natural hair textures.

She regularly flaunts her own tresses in both curls and straightened hairstyles. In her recent post, she gave her fans an inmate look at her model features with natural-looking makeup.

Proving just how well she can clean up her look after displaying her gray roots, Moore sported her signature straightened hair and looked glamorous sitting in a Bentley. In her caption, she wrote, "bow down."

Moore was dressed in a stylish sleeveless white top that was detailed with a white bow on the front. Her makeup included her signature thick lashes, smokey eyes, and a glossy pink lip.
