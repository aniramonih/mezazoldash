Sarah Spain Husband Brad Zibung Is A Real Estate Mogul In Chicago

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Sarah Spain husband Brad Zibung is a real estate agent. Zibung is an owner of the Heckler magazine from Chicago.

Sarah is a radio host and television personality who has won 'Emmy & Peabody' award as a Sports Journalist. She is also the owner of Chicago Red Stars.

The co-host of Spain and Fitz also worked as a co-host of the weekly show "Chicago's Best" on WGN TV. She also has an Ivy League education as a plus point in her career.

She also worked at the FSN and Fox sports before joining ESPN W in 2010. She has recently completed the last episode of 'Spain and Fitz' and is yet to announce her next step.

Brad Zibung Is A Chicago Businessman

Brad Zibung is a successful and experienced real estate agent at Compass.

Brad worked as a senior field operation manager for ten years at StubHub before changing his career in 2014. He is highly reputable in his career field as a real estate agent.

Zibung attended the University of Wisconsin at OshKosh where he studied Journalism and Public Relations for four years. He also worked as a Public Relation Assistant for two years during his college education.

After finishing his education at UOW, he worked at the Leo Burnett Group for five years as the Corporate Communications Associate. He established the fictional sports magazine 'The Heckler' in 2003.

The editor-in-chief of The Heckler, Brad is a huge baseball fan. He also owns different seasonal tickets for various clubs.

The philanthropist has also been actively involved in social work activities and has donated charities to many non-profit organizations like Peace for Pits, The Syrian Community Network, and Off The Street Club.

He has also helped the Chicago Hearing Society's initiative program, 'Hear the Cheers' co-founded by his wife Sarah.

The billionaire businessman is settled in Wicker Park Resident with his wife and two lovely dogs.

Inside Sarah Spain Married Life

Sarah and Zibung had an outdoor wedding in May 2016.

The duo tied the knot in the presence of their close friends and relatives in the beautiful view of nature at Toad Hollow Farm. They tagged each other as 'Ladyfriend' and 'Dreamboat' on their special day.

The happy pair have already spent six years of togetherness and are still going strong.

They have raised three pups, Haji, Banks, and Fletcher, in their house and are very fond of their company. The dogs even have their own Instagram account with 1,644 followers.

Zibung and Spain are sports lovers and support each other in their professional careers.

More on Sarah Spain Family

Sarah Spain parents are Rick Spain and Nancy Spain. Sarah has a sibling sister Katie Spain Peterson.

She was born on 18 August 1980 in Cleveland, Ohio and grew up in Lake Forest, Illinois.

The ESPN reporter attended Lake Forest High School and joined Cornell University for her further education. She is a Bachelor of Arts degree holder with a major subject as English.

She was the captain of the Varsity Track and Field Team during her college years. She was also a part of the National Society of Collegiate Scholars.

The columnist was a brilliant student during her childhood and often had excellent scoring during her terminal examinations. She was also equally good at English vocabulary, Literature, and demonstration.

Both Nancy and Rick are 70 years old in 2023. They are both adventure lovers and often goes to hiking with their daughters and son-in-law.

Katie is the mother of two kids and is often seen accompanying her sister and family during the holidays. Wes and Lizy are her niece and nephews. Besides, she is also a godmother of Vivienne Grace.
