FAQs about Grigory Sokolov

Who is Grigory Sokolov?

He is a distinguished Russian pianist who has earned international acclaim for his exceptional technique and musical empathy.

What are some highlights of Sokolov’s career?

He won the Gold Medal at the International Tchaikovsky Piano Competition in 1966 when he was just 16 years old. He is known for his unique approach to music, focusing on creating original, unrepeatable performances.

What is Sokolov’s repertoire like?

Sokolov’s repertoire spans a wide range of composers from various periods, including Bach, Beethoven, Schubert, Brahms, and 20th-century composers such as Prokofiev and Ravel. He also performs transcriptions of medieval sacred polyphony.

How does he approach live performances?

He is known for his deeply immersive live performances. He prefers to perform solo recitals and is not interested in recording in studios or performing with orchestras.

Does he give interviews or record albums?

What are some notable recordings of Grigory Sokolov?
