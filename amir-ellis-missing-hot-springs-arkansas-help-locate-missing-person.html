Amir Ellis Missing, Hot Springs Arkansas, Help Locate Missing Person

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 17, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Amir Ellis Missing – Although it has been eight months since Amir Ellis went missing, his family has now confirmed that the remains that were discovered along Highway 7 North were indeed those of their loved one. The mother of Amir Ellis, the man who vanished from Hot Springs eight months ago, and the Hot Springs Police Department have now verified that his body was discovered.

This comes after “possible human remains” were discovered off Highway 7 North in Garland County a little more than a week ago. The remains that were discovered were identified as belonging to Jessica Ellis’s kid, according to the investigators who spoke with her. Officers from the Hot Springs Police Department have stated that Amir Ellis was taken hostage in the month of May.

Since then, three individuals have been taken into custody for their possible role in his disappearance. Throughout the course of the past eight months, the Ellis family has been searching through the woods while grieving over Amir’s disappearance, his possible murder, and the court trials that have taken place. We will provide an update with additional information as soon as it is made available, but as of right now,

there is no information that has been made public indicating a possible cause of death. We attempted to get in touch with the police, but they informed us that they no longer have any information that can be made public at this time.
