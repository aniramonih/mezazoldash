Bonnie Wright is an English actress and filmmaker who is best known for portraying roles such as Ginny Weasley in the Harry Potter film series, Georgina in After the Dark, and Phoebe in Before I Sleep. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Agatha Christie: A Life in Pictures, King of the Hill, The Replacements, The Sea, A Christmas Carol, and many more. Her theatre credits include plays like The Moment of Truth. As a director and screenwriter, Bonnie has worked on projects such as Know Thyself, Medusa’s Ankles, and Separate We Come, Separate We Go. During the course of her career, she has been honored with several awards such as the Rodial Beautiful Award.
