Scott Menville Biography

Scott Menville is an American Actor, Voice Actor and Musician, who was born and brought-up in Malibu, California. He is widely known for his voice acting work in numerous animated films and television series which include, Duane in ‘Full House’, and the voice of Robin in ‘Teen Titans’, ‘Teen Titans: Trouble in Tokyo’, ‘Teen Titans Go! To the Movies’, and ‘Lego DC Batman: Family Matters’, among others.

Scott is also known for his voice acting roles in ‘Call of Duty: Black Ops II’ as Marlton Johnson, ‘Call of Duty: United Offensive’ as Pvt. Whitney, Pvt. Semashko, ‘Shaggy & Scooby-Doo Get a Clue!’ as Shaggy Rogers, Dr. Trebla, and in ‘Spider-Man’ as Dr. Otto Octavius / Doctor Octopus / Living Brain, Grady Scraps.
