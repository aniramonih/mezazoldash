Has Invasion Been Renewed For Season 3? Here's What We Know

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Invasion is an American science fiction television series that premiered on Apple TV+ in October 2021. The show follows the story of an alien invasion that threatens to wipe out humanity. The first season of Invasion was classified as a long setup for the next season by its creators. The second season of Invasion recently concluded, and fans are eager to know if the show will be renewed for a third season.

As of the latest information available, Invasion Season 3 has not been officially renewed. The show’s makers haven’t said if it will be renewed or when it will come out. However, fans of the show are hopeful that it will be renewed soon, as it is a hugely popular original series for Apple TV+. The show currently stands at the number three spot in the Apple TV+ charts, just behind The Morning Show and Ted Lasso, which makes for stiff competition.

Invasion Season 3 Renewal Status

Official Announcement

As of October 31, 2023, there has been no official announcement regarding the renewal or cancellation of Invasion for Season 3. The second season of the show has recently concluded, leaving fans wondering about the future of the series.

Predictions Based on Previous Seasons

Invasion has been a divisive show among viewers, with some praising its slow-burn storytelling and others criticizing its lack of action. The first season was classified as a setup for the next season, and the second season was expected to deliver on that promise. However, it seems that the second season failed to live up to expectations.

Based on the mixed reception of the second season and the lack of an official announcement, it is difficult to predict whether Invasion will be renewed for a third season. However, it is worth noting that Apple TV+ has a history of renewing its shows for multiple seasons, so there is still hope for fans of the series.

In conclusion, while there is currently no official announcement regarding the renewal or cancellation of Invasion for Season 3, it is possible that the show will be renewed based on Apple TV+’s history of renewing its shows. However, until an official announcement is made, fans will have to wait and see what the future holds for the series.

Potential Plot for Season 3

Continuation of Season 2’s Storyline

Season 2 of “Invasion” ended with a cliffhanger, leaving viewers wondering what will happen next. The season finale showed the aliens’ true intentions and how they plan to take over Earth. As the show’s renewal for Season 3 is still up in the air, fans are eagerly waiting to see how the story will progress.

If the show is renewed, it is expected that the storyline will continue from where it left off in Season 2. The aliens have already started to invade Earth, and it is likely that the next season will focus on the aftermath of the invasion. The show’s creator, Simon Kinberg, hinted that Season 3 would explore the consequences of the invasion and how humans will respond to it.

New Characters and Plot Twists

If “Invasion” is renewed for Season 3, it is expected that new characters will be introduced to the show. The previous season introduced several new characters, including the alien hybrids. It is possible that Season 3 will introduce new human characters who will play a significant role in the resistance against the aliens.

The show’s creator also hinted that Season 3 would have some plot twists that would surprise the viewers. As the aliens’ true intentions have been revealed, it is expected that the next season will have some unexpected turns that will keep the viewers on the edge of their seats.

Overall, if “Invasion” is renewed for Season 3, fans can expect to see a continuation of the show’s intense and thrilling storyline. However, until the show’s renewal is confirmed, fans will have to wait to see what happens next.
