Yung Joc

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Being a household name comes with its own set of peculiarities. Fans are always curious to learn about all aspects of their favorite celebrity, whether professional or personal.

Yung Joc, an American rapper, is no exception. Yung Joc’s professional life is well-known; however, the same cannot be said for his personal life. Yung Joc has a wife, several children, and several baby mamas.

Because having multiple children with different baby mamas does not imply that the rapper is married to all of them, the question of how many times he was married arises.

Let’s take a closer look and see what we can find out!

Net worth of Yung Joc

 Yung Joc has estimated net worth is to be a $4 million.

Yung Joc’s Married Life and Romantic Affair with His Children’s Mother:

In the year 2001, the American rapper Yung Joc married his high school sweetheart, Alexandria Robinson, and made his then-girlfriend a wife. Joc and Alexandra have three children together: Ja’Kori, a daughter, and Amir and Chase, two sons.

Joc’s 13-year marriage came to an end in 2014 when his ex-wife, Alexandria, filed for divorce, citing adultery as the reason for the divorce. Yung Joc’s ex-wife, Alexandria, was fed up with him dating Karlie Redd, who was also a contestant on the show ‘Love & Hip Hop Atlanta’ at the time. On the show, Yung was living with Karlie Redd at the time.

Meanwhile, Yung’s ex-wife revealed that the couple had been divorced in February 2012. She also requested spousal and child support when she filed for divorce.

Aside from the children with his ex-wife, Alexandria, He has five more children with three different baby mamas.

Joc has a 17-year-old son named Amoni, whom he shares with his baby mother, Fatimah.

Similarly, he has twins Cadence and Camora (4 years old) with Carla, his baby mother.

Similarly, Joc has a twin sister, Eden, and Allon (3 years old) has a baby mother, Sina.

Despite this, Joc has not revealed the timeline of his relationships with the other three baby mamas, with the exception of his ex-wife. Not only that, but he has remained tight-lipped about whether he is dating any of  the baby mamas or has reconciled with his ex-wife. As a result, the situation is still unclear!
