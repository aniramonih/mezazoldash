Eminem's Adopted Daughter Whitney Scott Mathers Biological Father

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Almost everybody knows Whitney Scott Mathers. She is the adopted daughter of Eminem aka Marshall Bruce Mathers III.

So, who is the biological father of Whitney? Without any suspense, his name is Eric Hartter. Want to know more about him scroll below. 

Is Eric Hartter Alive? Early Life And Death

Eric Hartter was born in the 1980s in the United States of America to mother Marie Hartter and her husband. He grew up in his hometown with his parents. 

Born in the 1980s, he died in the March of 2020 the Pop Culture reported. According to the Detroit Police Department, Eric was found dead in an abandoned house located in Detroit and was pronounced DOA on the scene. 

As per the same source: "A call was made by a family member who came by to check the location, and found the victim unresponsive with no signs of trauma."

The police department cited the reason for the 40 years old Eric died due to a drug overdose. Moreover, his family members disclosed that Eric Hartter struggled with drug addiction for years and was recently indulged in heroin abuse. 

Nationality, Height, And Weight

Eric Hartter held an American nationality and belongs to white ethnic background. He had a weight of 5 feet 10 inches (177 cm) and weighs around 76 kg (167 pounds). 

Eric Hartter Career: Tattoo Artist And Criminal Activities

Eric Hartter was a tattoo artist and a famous one. As per the source, he was a celebrated tattoo artist and has tatted many Hollywood stars. 

Later, Hartter was also accused of being a drug dealer. He used to run a drug cartel business under his tattoo work. The information of him running an illegal drug cartel was tipped to police and was arrested for drug possession. 

Moreover, he was also caught stealing a Red Bull can worth $200 from the CVS on 27 December 2018. As a matter of fact, he came to the headlines after he came into a romantic relationship with Kim Mathers, Eminem’s Ex-wife. 

Dated Eminem’s Ex-Wife Kimberly Mathers: Personal Life and Daughter

Not only for his controversial career, but Eric Hartter was also known for his love life. He started dating Kim Mathers in 2001. The couple was blessed with an adorable daughter, Whitney Scott Mathers. She was born on 16 April 2002. 

Eric and Kim separated due to his legal issues. Kim went back to Eminem and they re-married in 2006. After marrying, their relationship only lasted for 3 months. Soon, Eminem adopted Eric’s daughter legally. He raised his adopted daughter like his own. 

Though Hartter was the biological father of Whitney, he never got the chance to see her. 

His Daughter Whitney Scott Mathers

Whitney Scott Mathers was born in 2002 and adopted by Eminem in 2005. She grew up with her siblings: Hailie Jade and Alaina Marie Mathers. She holds an American nationality and belongs to white ethnic background. 

Whitney is 18 years old. She has the height of 5 feet 3 inches (160 cm) and weighs around 55 kg (121 pounds). 

On 11 October 2017, Whitney Scott Mathers confessed that she is bisexual through an Instagram post waving a rainbow flag. The rainbow flag represents the LGBTQ+ community.
