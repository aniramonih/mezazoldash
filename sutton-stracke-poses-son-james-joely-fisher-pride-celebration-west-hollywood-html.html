Sutton Stracke poses with her son James and Joely Fisher at her Pride celebration in West Hollywood

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Sutton Stracke took a break from clashing with Diana Jenkins on Real Housewives of Beverly Hills to host a Pride celebration on Thursday, the last day of Pride Month.

The 50-year-old party host posed with her son James, actress Joely Fisher and more at the celebration, held at her fashion boutique Sutton in West Hollywood.

They were joined by others such as actor Felix Solis, Terry Hu, Amelia McCarthy, Nancy Davis and Jai Rodriguez.

She also rocked a flowery barrette with her blonde locks falling just below her shoulders for the event.
