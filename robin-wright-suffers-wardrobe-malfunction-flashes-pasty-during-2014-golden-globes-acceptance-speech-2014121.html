Robin Wright's Golden Globes Wardrobe Malfunction, Flashes Pasty: Vid

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Watch those pasties! Robin Wright gave fans quite the eyeful while accepting the award for Best Actress in a Television Series for her role as Claire Underwood in Netflix’s House of Cards. 

The stunned actress recalled Merritt Wever‘s 2013 Emmy Awards speech in which the Nurse Jackie actress said, “I’ve got to go, bye!” The newly-engaged Wright, 47, motioned backstage saying she wished she could join Wever. And as she motioned behind her, her gold-beaded Reem Acra halter gown shifted, showing off the flesh-colored pasties covering her nipple.
