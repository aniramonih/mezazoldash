Why Did John Fury Go To Prison? What Is His Conviction?

John Fury has a criminal record in the past as he had served four years in prison. He was convicted of aggressively gouging out Oathie Skyes’s eye that has left him half-blind.

Per the reports, the duo started a nasty fight in a Cyprus bar in 1999. Their grudge lasted long for 12 years and in 2010 when they met again at a car auction, Fury had grasped Skyes’s one eye.

Fury had even opted to take another eye but failed and was tried in court. In the court, Fury had requested the judge to pardon him as his son Tyson’s boxing career had just taken off.

The court had sentenced him to 11 years in prison but Fury got out in four years in 2015 with a plea of leniency.
