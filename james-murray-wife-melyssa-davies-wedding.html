Who Is James Murray's Wife Melyssa Davies? Their Wedding Details

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Melyssa Davies just walked down the aisle with her boyfriend-cum-husband, James Murray. People confirmed the wedding of Davies with the 'Impractical' star on Saturday on 26 September 2020. The source mentioned that the lovebirds tied a knot on 25 September 2020 in an outdoor ceremony at Lake House Inn in Bucks County, Pennsylvania.

Several guests, including "Jokers" Sal Vulcano, Brian Quinn, and Joe Gatto, attended the ceremony, where masks were a mandatory thing. Joe Gatto officiated the lovely wedding of Melyssa Davies and her husband, James Murray, where there were stations for both masks and sanitizing, in response to the ongoing pandemic, COVID-19.

Davies and Murray's original plan for the wedding got altered due to the pandemic. Concerning the scheduled marriage, Murray mentioned:

"We had four different weddings being planned simultaneously, depending on how everything worked. We had four different wedding lists and four different plans, and one version of it was just our bridal party in our backyard, at the most extreme. The two ultimately compromised and invited about a quarter of their original guests, which Staten Islander Murray said was “a lot less stress … It’s a lot less moving parts in that way."

Similarly, Murray further assured that both he and his wife are healthy and safe. 'It was kind [of] a blessing in that way, having this time to live together and plan our lives together,' he added.

Likewise, Murray shared a wedding picture on his Instagram on 26 September 2020 and captioned, 'Mr. & Mrs.'

Melyssa Davies donned in a white sleeveless floral gown while Murray appeared in a black wedding tuxedo as they said, 'I do.'

The newlywed lovebirds are set to enjoy their honeymoon in the Maldives, at a five-star Soneva Jani resort. It had always been a dream to travel to the Maldives. Murray said he 'had resigned me to knowing that I wouldn't go because you wouldn't go to this place unless you were getting married.'

Who Is Melyssa Davies?

Melyssa Davies is a newlywed wife of the 44-year-old American comedian, James Murray. She is a nurse by profession, who finished her nursing course before getting engaged to Murray. Besides, she leads a modest lifestyle, which has overshadowed her personal details.

Melyssa Davies and James Murray (©: Instagram/James Murray)

She is an animal lover and has a dog named Penelope. Penelope has an Instagram account with over 6.5k followers, even more than Melyssa herself, who enriches nearly 3k followers on the platform.

Melyssa Davies and James Murray first met at 'Awakened' launched the party in New York back in June 2018. The lovely pair dated for over a year and got engaged on 30 August 2020 in New Jersey. Murray opened about his engagement on Instagram, citing 'Best day of my life.'

Since then, the lovebirds have been committed to each other, and recently, they took their relationship to the new level.
