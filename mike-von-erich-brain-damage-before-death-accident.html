Furthermore, Mike competed with many wrestlers like Sjandor Akbar and Michael Hays. As a tag team with his brother, the team competed with different teams like  The Freddbirds, Terry Gordy, and Buddy Roberts.

Moreover, the athlete was a part of the New Japan Pro Wrestling tour in  January 1987. He teamed up with many superstars like Tony St. Clair, Black Bart, and Konga The Barbarian and competed against Antonio Inoki and Tatsumi Fujinamu.

Sadly, Mike Von Erich passed away on 12 April 1987. He was just 23 at the time of his demise.
