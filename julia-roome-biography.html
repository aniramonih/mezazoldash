Who Is Julia Roome?

Julia Roome is an American singer and songwriter who became popular after appearing on The Voice, season 24. She appeared as the youngest competitor on the stage of The Voice. She made the hit on the stage with her incredible voice.

Julia Roome – Professional Life, Careers

Talking about her career, Julia Roome built a keen interest in singing from an early age. She built amazing musical skills since she was in grade second. At the age of 7, she sang her first song with a self-taught ukulele. She released her first original single called Steel Glass when she was only 10 years old.

Moreover, At the age of 11, she released her original EP “I’m Gunna” which was able to place at number 9 on the iTunes Charts. After that, she did a music tour and turned her career into a professional one at the age of 12.

She went on a music tour in several places such as East Coast from New York to Key West Florida, Nashville, Georgia, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey. She performed as many as she could or that could fit her schedule. Julia received a huge limelight after she sang the National Anthem and after she appeared on the show, The Voice.

Julia sang the 1931 classic “Dream a Little Dream of Me on the stage of The Voice, season 24. She successfully impressed The judges, Niall Horan and Gwen Stefani turned their chairs. She became the youngest contestant on the stage of The Voice Season 24 to be the youngest one to be sent to the next round.
