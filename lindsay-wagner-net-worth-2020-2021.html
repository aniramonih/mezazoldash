Early life

The American actress was born in Los Angeles, California, on June 22, 1949, to William Wagner and Marilyn Wagner. When Lindsay was only seven years old, her parents got separated, which led her to move with her mother to Eagle Rock, Pasadena.

Following that, her mother married Ted Ball, and Lindsay had to move Portland, Oregon, with her parents. She attended Davis Douglas High School there and then enrolled at the University of Oregon. Later, she also attended Mount Hood Community College briefly.
