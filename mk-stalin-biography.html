MK Stalin Biography

MK Stalin Biography: - MK Stalin was born on 1 March 1953, in Madras, Madras State, India. He is 70 years old. Stalin is the third son of M. Karunanidhi, the second Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu and the leader of the DMK, and his wife, Dayalu Ammal. Karunanidhi was speaking at a condolence meeting for Soviet leader Joseph Stalin, who died barely four days after his kid was born, and hence named his son Stalin. Stalin went to the Madras Christian College Higher Secondary School for his education. He attended Vivekananda College for pre-university studies before earning a history degree from Presidency College, Chennai, of the University of Madras in 1973.
