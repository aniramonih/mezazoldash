Early Life

Scarface was born in the year 1970. He went to study at Woodson Middle School in Houston, located in Texas. His upbringing was done as a Christian; however, he got transformed into Islam in the year 2006.

He is known to be a cousin of a singer named Johnny Nash. Glancing at his personal life, he faced severe depression and got detected with bipolar disorder when he was growing up. This disorder directed him to try suicide by injuring his wrists and also did many drug overdoses throughout his youth.

Career

Scarface kicked off his career as a rapper by working as DJ Akshen recording solo meant for Lil’ Troy’s Short Stop Records, identified as a native label within Houston. Later his career boosted when he signed with Rap-A-Lot Records and partnered with a group who were jointly identified as Geto Boys substituting one partner who left and launched the second album of the group entitled Grip It! On That Other Level in the year 1989.

As an album entitled Mr. Scarface Is Back became a success, fame of Scarface dominated over remaining Geto Boys. During the year 2002, the rapper has launched The Fix, identified as the addition to The Last of a Dying Breed, and came back to the studio through the Geto Boys for its album entitled The Foundation.

Other than his major career as a rapper, he has, too, stayed as a coordinator as well as a president of Def Jam South from the year 2000, where he has promoted the career of a famous rapper named Ludacris, the one whom he first signed to the label.
