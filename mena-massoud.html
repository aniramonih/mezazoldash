Career

Mena Massoud made his acting debut in 2011 with a role in the television series ‘Nikita.’ The same year, he was cast in three different series, including ‘Poser,’ ‘Combat Hospital,’ and ‘The 99.’ Similarly, in 2012, he appeared in two TV shows, the first as Jason in ‘Cut to the Chase’ and the second as Malik Atassi in ‘King.’ After three years in the 2015 mystery-drama series ‘Open Heart,’ he made his television debut as Jared Malik. Similarly, he appeared as Justin in an episode of ‘Saving Hope’ in 2017 and as Tarek in six episodes of ‘Jack Ryan’ in 2018. In addition, he recently appeared in the 2019 series ‘Reprisal’ as Ethan. He played a prominent role in this series, sharing the screen with actors such as Abigail Spencer, Rodrigo Santoro, Shane Callahan, and others.

Mena Massoud made his film debut in 2011 as Jad in ‘What Happens Next.’ Following that, he appeared in films such as ‘Americanistan’ in 2014, ‘Let’s Rap in 2015, ‘Ordinary Days’ in 2017, and ‘Masters in Crime’ in 2018. Similarly, in 2019, he received his first significant role in the Disney fantasy film ‘Aladdin,’ in which he played the titular character.
Will Smith, Naomi Scott, Marwan Kenzari, Nasim Pedrad, Billy Magnussen, and Navid Negahban also appeared in the film. Despite receiving mixed reviews, this film became the ninth highest-grossing film. In addition, the same year, he was cast as Chaz in the thriller film ‘Strange but True.’ His most recent films in 2020 were ‘Run This Town’ and ‘Lamyas’a Poem,’ in which he played Rumi’s voice.
