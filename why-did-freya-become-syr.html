Why did Freya become SYR?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Freya became SYR as part of a deal with a little girl named Horn. By making this deal, Freya obtained the ability to transform into SYR, while Horn gained the ability to transform into Freya.

Syr was originally the name belonging to Horn. After making a deal with the little girl, Freya obtained her name and the ability to transform into her, while Horn gained the ability to transform into Freya.

Why did Freya disguise as SYR?

Syr was picked up as a child by Freya and wished to be Freya. So Freya took the identity of Syr for herself, and gave Syr (who was renamed Helun) transformation magic to become Freya. Basically, whenever we see Syr in the series, it’s actually Freya, and during those times Helun transforms and takes the place of Freya.

Does Ryu know Syr is Freya?

Upon finally learning Syr’s true identity as Freya, Ryu was shocked and denied that she was truly Syr when the Goddess showed an attitude unbefitting her friend’s usual one. Their relationship returned back to normal after Ryu and the rest of waitresses convinced Freya to stay in Orario as Syr following the War Game.

Is Syr in love with Bell?

They both are in love with him, but Ryu is still in a little bit of a denial phase and still tries to pair Bell and Syr together.

Why is Freya so obsessed with Bell?

On the surface, Freya simply wants Bell to become one of her prized possessions, to sit by her side as a showpiece to further her stance as Orario’s strongest. However, her desire for Bell goes far beyond the simplistic view of attaining his love, since she is primarily motivated by her destiny to find her husband.

Is Syr really the Goddess Freya?

Freya initially used the identity of Syr just as a disguise, a mere game of roleplay like the ones she played in Heaven. Over time, however, her attitude and feelings became more and more genuine, to the point Syr became “Freya without makeup”, a reflection of her real self.
