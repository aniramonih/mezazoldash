Willie Spence funeral, date, time, venue, obituary poster

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 17, 2024

The famous Willie Spence was an American singer. He was a runner-up of Season 19 of American Idol at the age of 22.

Willie Spence was born on June 18, 1999, and he died on October 11, 2022.

Spence died in a car accident on Interstate 24 near Chattanooga on October 11, 2022. His death was reported by the Marion County Sheriff’s Office. According to WSB-TV in Atlanta, he was driving a Jeep Cherokee when he veered off I-24 at around 16:00 ET. His vehicle struck a car that was stopped on the shoulder of the road. The driver of that vehicle was not injured.

Spence’s unexpected death drew a large outpouring of sympathy. The official Instagram account for American Idol reposted a video of Spence’s audition for the show, writing We are devastated about the passing of our beloved American Idol family member, Willie Spence. He was a true talent who lit up every room he entered and will be deeply missed. We send our condolences to his loved ones.

Chayce Beckham, the winner of Season 19 of American Idol, posted a tribute on Instagram to the man he had bested that year. “He had a laugh that could make you melt, honest humor you couldn’t help but love, and the biggest heart you could imagine all wrapped up into one human. Katharine McPhee, a former contestant on Season 5 of American Idol, who had also previously performed with Spence, also took to Instagram to express her sadness over Spence’s death.
