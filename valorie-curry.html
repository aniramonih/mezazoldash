The Following Actress Valorie Curry Has Tied The Knot With Actor Husband, Sam Underwood in 2016

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 15, 2024

American actress, Valorie Curry came to the limelight after portraying the role of Jane Kuhne in the teen noir mystery drama TV series, Veronica Mars from 2005 to 2006. It ran from September 22, 2004, to July 19, 2019, on UPN (seasons 1–2), The CW (season 3), Hulu (season 4).

Further, she was the cast of the 2012 romantic fantasy film The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2, playing the role of Charlotte. Today we are discussing more details about her so read the entire article. 

Who is Valorie Curry Husband?

35 years old actress Valorie is in a marital relationship with her husband, Sam Underwood, 33. He is an actor who's starring as Adam Carrington on The CW and Netflix drama, Dynasty, starring Nathalie Kelley. Also, he is famous as  Jake Otto in Fear the Walking Dead.

They first met in 2013 on the set of the crime drama television series The Following. She portrayed Emma Hill in the show, while Underwood portrayed Luke and Mark Gray, the twins. They got closer while working together and began dating in 2015.

After one year of love romance, Sam proposed to her with a beautiful ring in 2016. Finally, they exchanged the vows on the 13th of May 2016 in a special ceremony. Their big day took place in front of their loved ones. 

Since then, the duo has been enjoying their blissful marital relationship for five years. Moreover, Valorie frequently shared beautiful pictures of them on her Instagram account. Apart from her personal life, she came out as a pansexual in 2019.

How Much is Valorie Curry Net Worth?

As of June 2021, Valorie Curry has a staggering net worth of $500 Thousand, as per some sources. She has accumulated her fortune throughout her professional acting work. Since 2005, she has worked in the film industry.

For her salary, Curry reportedly makes $40,000 per year. Likewise, she made her film debut with the short film, I Love You Like Crazy as Melody in 2011. Her romantic fantasy Bill Condon's film The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2 has made $830 million worldwide against the $136 million budget in 2012.

Along with this, she has worked in several movies like American Pastoral, Blair Witch, After Darkness, and Inherit the Viper. On the other side, she made her TV debut noir mystery drama, Veronica Mars as Jane Kuhne in six episodes. It was narrated by Kristen Bell as Veronica Mars.

She moreover has featured in television series such as CSI: NY (2011), Psych (2012), The Following (2013–14), House of Lies (2015–16), and The Tick (2016–19). And her upcoming series is Langdon where she would play the role of Katherine Solomon. In 2018, she played the role of Kara in the music video, Detroit: Become Human.

Valorie Curry's Bio, Education and Early Life

Valorie Curry was born on 12th February 1986 in Orange County, California, the United States as Valorie Mae Curry. Her parents are Edwin B. Curry and Karen V. Fix.

She has two siblings, a brother David Curry and a sister, Colleen Curry. So far, she graduated from Sonora High School in 2004. Later she received a degree in theater from the California State University, Fullerton.
