Cree Summer Biography, Husband, Net Worth and Other Highlights

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Cree Summer is one of those people you won’t find missing from any list that ranks the best voice actresses in Hollywood. Also a singer and actress, she began her career in 1983 and has since appeared in hundreds of animated films, animated series, live-action films and video games. More so, she has several music albums.

Cree Summer Biography

The American-Canadian actress was born CreeSummer Francks on July 7, 1969 in Los Angeles, California. However, it was on the Red Pheasant Reservation in Saskatchewan, Canada that she was raised by her parents; Don Francks, Canadian actor and musician, and Lili Clark, dancer and actress.

Raised alongside her brother, actor and singer Rainbow Sun Francks, born ten years after her, Summer did not go to school early as she spent most of her time on the reserve with her parents where she grew up. singing and dancing. Interestingly, it was reported that she never saw television until she was 8 years old. When the family finally moved to town around age 9, she attended public school.

Adjusting to school was very difficult for the summer because she had never been to school before and had never been exposed to such activities. Because of this, she decided to quit her studies when she was only 16 years old. Back then, she had her own car and all she did with her friends was go on adventures.

Cree began her acting career in 1983 when she had a role in Inspector Gadget as Penny. Her voice was so unique that in no time she was able to land roles in everything from movies to TV series to video games. She voiced the character Orange Blossom in Strawberry Shortcake and the Nameless Baby (1984), Hecate in Hellboy: Blood and Iron (2007), and Cheetah in Lego DC Comics Super Heroes: Justice League – Attack of the Legion of Doom (2015).

Some series she became part of are Star Wars: Droids and Ewoks from 1986 to 1987, The Incredible Hulk (1997), and Guardians of the Galaxy (2015). On the video game front, she’s appeared in games like Tiny Toon Adventures: Buster and the Beanstalk (1996), X-Men Legends (2004), and Lego DC Super-Villains (2018).

Husband

As Cree Summer hasn’t revealed much about her relationship, it is known that she is married to Angelo Pullen. It’s unclear exactly when they got married, but the two have been together for many years.

According to his profile, Angelo Pullen works in finance, marketing and digital. He is the co-founder of 3BLACKDOT, an entertainment company based in Los Angeles. The couple are blessed with three children; Miles Summer-Pullen, born in 1998, Brave Littlewing Pullens, born in 2011 and Hero Peregrine Pullens, born in 2013.
