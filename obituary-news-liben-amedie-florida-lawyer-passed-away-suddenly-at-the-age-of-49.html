News of the death of Liben company lawyer passed away

After the sad passing, the legal community mourns the loss of Liben Amedie, a prominent figure in Real Estate Law and Business Law. At the age of 49, attorney Liben Amedie, the esteemed founder of the Liben Law Firm, bid farewell to this world, leaving behind a 17-year legacy that deeply impacted both his colleagues and the legal community at large.

Born in 1974 in Tampa, Florida, Liben Amedie attended Jefferson High School and graduated with honors in 1992. He then received a full scholarship to study at the University of Florida, where he graduated with a Bachelor of Laws degree in 1996.
