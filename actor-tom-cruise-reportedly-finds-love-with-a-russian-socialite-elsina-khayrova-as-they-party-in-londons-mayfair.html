Actor Tom Cruise reportedly finds love with a Russian socialite Elsina Khayrova as they party in Lon

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Actor Tom Cruise has reportedly found love with a Russian socialite, Elsina Khayrova, as they were seen partying in London’s Mayfair. Cruise, 61, was spotted at a party in Mayfair with socialite Elsina Khayrova, 36, who is the daughter of a prominent Russian MP and the ex-wife of a diamond-trading oligarch.

“They were inseparable, clearly a couple,” one guest at the almost entirely Russian event told the Daily Mail. “He seemed to be besotted with her.”

The pair arrived at the party in Grosvenor Square together and reportedly left the event together in the early hours. Miss Khayrova, dressed in a strapless black dress with a diamanté bow, was seen on the dance floor with the actor. They posed for pictures with Avramenko Aliena, a former Miss Europe contestant, before hitting the dance floor together.

Miss Khayrova, a former model and a British citizen, was previously married to Russian tycoon Dmitry Tsetkov, with whom she shared a £22million mansion in Surrey, as well as houses in London worth £18million. The couple also owned properties in Dubai and Cyprus, along with car, jewelry, and art collections worth millions of pounds.

Their divorce made headlines after Miss Khayrova was accused of hiding substantial assets from her ex-husband, including her £1million handbag collection.
