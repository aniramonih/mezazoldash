Is Liz Gillies Pregnant? A Closer Look at Recent Speculations and What We Really Know!

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 9, 2024

Is Liz Gillies pregnant? In the ever-curious realm of celebrity gossip, the spotlight has recently turned to the talented Elizabeth “Liz” Gillies.

Known for her roles as Jade West in Victorious and Fallon Carrington in Dynasty, Gillies has seamlessly transitioned between acting and singing, even gracing Broadway at the young age of 15.

Her musical prowess extended to her debut album, “We Wish You the Merriest,” a festive collaboration with Seth MacFarlane, released just this November.

As Liz Gillies made a lively appearance on the Today Show to promote her latest musical venture, questions about her personal life have resurfaced.

The rumor mill is buzzing with speculation that the actress and singer might be expecting. The burning question on everyone’s mind: Is Liz Gillies pregnant? Let’s delve into the details and separate fact from fiction regarding this intriguing piece of celebrity gossip.

Is Liz Gillies Pregnant?

No, Liz Gillies is not pregnant. As of now, there’s absolutely no concrete evidence to support the idea that she’s expecting. Not a single official statement or baby bump sighting is to be found.

I mean, we’ve all become quite the detectives, right? Scouring through Instagram, checking out the latest pictures, and guess what? No visible baby bump in sight.

It seems like these rumors are floating in a sea of baseless speculations. Liz hasn’t dropped any pregnancy bombshells, and until she does, it’s safe to say this is just another case of the rumor mill working overtime.

So, in a nutshell, the verdict is in – Liz Gillies is not pregnant, at least not according to the evidence available to us mere mortals. Time to put those rumors to rest!

Who Is Elizabeth “Liz” Gillies Married To?

Elizabeth “Liz” Gillies tied the knot with American music producer Michael Corcoran on August 8, 2020. The couple exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony held in Chesterfield Township, New Jersey, sealing their romantic journey that began back in late 2012.

Fate played its part, bringing them together on the set of the Nickelodeon show Victorious. Currently residing in the heart of Atlanta,

Georgia, Liz, and Michael have built a life filled with love and shared dreams. Reflecting on their early days, Gillies shared in an interview with Vogue Magazine, “I always had a crush on him. But I played it cool.”

It seems that their love story, which started on the small screen, has blossomed into a beautiful reality that extends far beyond the glitz and glamour of Hollywood.

Liz Gilies’ Husband: Meet Michael Corcoran!

Meet Michael Corcoran, the man of many musical talents known professionally as Backhouse Mike or Ken Lofkoll. This American record producer, composer, and musician has left his mark on the entertainment scene with a string of notable achievements.

His compositions have graced popular Nickelodeon shows such as Drake & Josh, iCarly, Victorious, The Troop, Sam & Cat, and Henry Danger. Disney Channel favorites Shake It Up and Liv and Maddie have also benefited from his musical touch, along with Netflix’s The Mr., Peabody & Sherman Show and VH1’s Hit the Floor.

Clearly, Corcoran’s musical prowess extends across various platforms, leaving an indelible imprint on the soundtracks of our favorite shows.
