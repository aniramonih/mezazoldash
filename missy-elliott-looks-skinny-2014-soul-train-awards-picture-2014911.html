Missy Elliott Looks Skinny at 2014 Soul Train Awards: Picture

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Work it! Missy Elliott hit up the 2014 Soul Train Awards in Las Vegas on Friday, Nov. 7, wowing the crowd when she reunited with Lil’ Kim and Da Brat to sing their 1996 platinum single “Ladies Night” as a tribute to Kool & the Gang. 

Even in a baggy bejeweled hood and leather pants, the legendary emcee, 43, was able to show off her 30-pound weight looks, looking fit and trim.
