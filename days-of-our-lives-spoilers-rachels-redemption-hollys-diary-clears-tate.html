Rachels Redemption, Hollys Diary Clears Tate?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Days of Our Lives spoilers reveal that Tate Black (Jamie Martin Mann) has been sent to rehab in Illinois. However, fans know that Tate doesn’t do drugs and wasn’t responsible for Holly Jonas’ (Ashley Puzemis) overdose.

Could Rachel Black (Finley Rose Slater) earn redemption by exposing a secret? It wouldn’t surprise us if Rachel not only knew where Holly hides her diary but has been reading it as well. Could Holly’s diary clear Tate’s name?

Holly Jonas’ Overdose

DOOL spoilers reveal that Holly overdosed on drugs on New Year’s Eve. While Holly is in a coma and on life support, EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel) is making Tate pay the price.

Fans already know that Tate doesn’t do drugs and refused Holly’s offer to take pills. Then, Holly ran off and the next thing Tate knew, she was on the floor having an overdose.

Even though Holly hasn’t woken up yet, she is being transferred to a Rome clinic. It is the same clinic that helped EJ during his own recovery.

DOOL Spoilers: Kristen DiMera’s Promise

While Nicole Walker-DiMera (Arianne Zucker) is in Rome with Holly, EJ promised that he would get justice in the teenager’s name. Meanwhile, Kristen DiMera (Stacy Haiduk) blasted Brady Black (Eric Martsolf) for missing his date with Rachel.

After Brady explained what happened to Holly and Tate, Kristen softened. Kristen assured Brady she would tell Rachel what happened with Holly and Tate.

Kristen mentioned that Rachel is fond of Tate. The same can’t be said for Rachel and Holly’s relationship, though.

Brady Black’s Make-Up Date

Days of Our Lives spoilers for the week of January 22 reveal that Brady will have a make-up date with Rachel and Kristen. Brady hasn’t seen Rachel since Holly and Tate’s disastrous New Year’s Eve.

So, Rachel might have some questions about what is going to happen to Tate. When Brady tries to explain that Tate is in rehab although he doesn’t do drugs, the little girl will know this isn’t right.

Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Rachel Black’s Redemption

Even though Rachel and Holly bickered, they could know some of each other’s secrets. At one time, Kristen had to negotiate a peace treaty between the girls over shampoo.

Remember, Rachel seems to know lots of intel. That’s why Stefan DiMera (Brandon Barash) kept playing the questions game with Rachel.

It’s possible that Rachel can clear Tate. A lot of teenage girls keep diaries. It would be just like Rachel to not only know Holly’s diary location, but she’s probably read some of the entries. Could a little girl end up clearing Tate’s name?
