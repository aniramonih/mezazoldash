    The hugely popular franchise is back on 20 November, and Mockingjay follows on from the end of , when Katniss destroyed the stadium in which the 75th Hunger Games were being held.

    She was collected by the mysterious and previously unknown inhabitants of District 13, which was thought to have been destroyed at the end of the first rebellion against the Capitol years before.

    Jennifer Lawrence as Katniss Everdeen

    In this new teaser trailer, Katniss is forced to return to her home in District 12 by the rebels, only to discover it has been destroyed by the Capitol. The rebels hope that the shock will make her understand the battle they have in front of them.

    New Zealand singer-songwriter Lorde has curated the soundtrack to the film and her single is played over the top of this clip.Jennifer returns to the film along with Liam Hemsworth as Gale Hawthorne who is now a member of the rebels, and Josh Hutcherson as Peeta Mellark, whom Katniss learns is alive after he was left in the ruins of the previous Games.
