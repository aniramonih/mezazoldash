Gidle: Must Know Facts about the Cube Entertainment girls Group

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Gidle is a girl group formed by Cube Entertainment which based at South Korea. The the group’s mini-album titled I am, with the title track Latata, the group debuted on May 2, 2018.

The group was made up by five hardworking boys namely Miyeon, Minnie, Soyeon, Yuqi and Shuhua.

However, on August 14, 2021, the public was alerted on the need for Soobin to leave the Idle group due to the controversies associated with him. Cube Entertainment made this announcement.

Gidle members

Miyeon

Miyeon took birth at Incheon South Korea on January 31, 1997. He is the main vocalist and visual of the group. Miyeon stands at a height of 5 feet 4 inches tall and weighs at 45 kilograms

Minnie

Minnie is the main vocalist of the Idle group. He was born on October 23, 1997 in Bangkok, Thailand and stands at a height of 5 feet 6 inches tall and weighs at 45 kilograms.

Soyeon

Soyeon holds the office of the leader, main rapper, sub vocalist and center of the group. He was born in Seoul, South Korea on August 26, 1998 and stands at a height of 5 feet 2 inches tall and weighs at 44 kilograms

Yuqi

Yuqi served as the lead dancer, sub vocalist, sub rapper and the Face of the Idle group. He was born on September 23, 1999 in Beijing, China. Yuqi stands at a height of 5 feet 4 inches tall and weighs at 43 kilograms

Shuhua

Shuhua was born on January 6, 2000 in Taoyuan, Taiwan. He stands at a height of 5 feet 4 inches tall and weighs at 45 kilograms. He is the sub vocalist, visual and Maknae of the group.
