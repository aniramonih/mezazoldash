Joseph Frontiera is an American television personality, well known for his appearance in the reality TV series, Counting Cars. He became more famous after a lawsuit filed against him.

Frontiera was born on 1st July 1988 in the United States of America, where another television star Crystal Harris was born. Born to his American parents, he is of White ethnicity and holds an American nationality.

Lawsuits Against Joseph For Fraud

The famous star from Counting Cars used to work as a car mechanic before the limelight. On 13 August 2012, Joseph made his first appearance in the television show titled Counting Cars aired via History Network. The show is the third spin-off of the reality TV series Pawn Stars.

Counting Stars has filmed the ninth season. In the show, Frontiera appeared with various stars such as Danny Koker, Mike Henry, Shannon Aikau, Rick Harrison, Big Ryan, Kevin Mack, etc. 

Everything was going fine until he became a part of a controversy when the Count's Kustoms, an automobile restoration and customization company owned by Danny Koker filed a lawsuit against him. In 2017, the company claimed that Joseph spent $75,000 from the company's assets for his personal use. Joseph was also accused of using $18,000 for a down payment of his Land Rover and plane tickets. 

According to his records from the Las Vegas Justice Court, Joesph was charged $3,500 for felony theft. Not only that but he had also a criminal history of fraud with a past employer in Florida. Resulting in the accusation, Joseph also lost his work to appear in the series. As of now, his name is also not listed as the cast member of Counting Cars.
