Joe Musgrove Wiki/Biography, Age, Education

Joe Musgrove was born on 4 December 1992 in El Cajon, California, the United States of America, he is 29 years old now. Joe started participating in baseball tournaments at the age of 7, and he broke out as a star pitcher and batter during his junior season at Grossmont High School in San Diego’s east county, California, where he helped take him to 2 state championship appearances.

Joe attended Grossmont High School in El Cajon, in San Diego’s east county where he emerged as a strong two-way player during his junior season. As a pitcher in 2010, Joe boasted an 11–1 win-loss record with 5 saves.

On 19 January 2021, the Pittsburgh Pirates traded Joe to his hometown San Diego Padres in a three-team trade and On 1 August 2022, the San Diego Padres announced they signed Joe to a five-year contract extension, worth $100 million
