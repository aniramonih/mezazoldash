Malcolm Gladwell parents: Joyce Gladwell, Graham Gladwell

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

A well-known British journalist, author, and public speaker, Malcolm Timothy Gladwell has worked at the New Yorker for almost three decades.

Gladwell’s best writings are now being recognized, and as a result, many of his readers have flocked to social media to learn more about his family, particularly his true mother and father.

Malcolm Gladwell parents: Joyce Gladwell, Graham Gladwell

For three decades, Malcolm Gladwell has been a contributing author at The New Yorker.

Also, he is a co-founder and president of the audio production business Pushkin Industries, which also publishes his most recent audiobook, (Miracle and Wonder,) a biography of Paul Simon, as well as the well-known podcast (Revisionist History).

Joyce Gladwell has been said to be his biological mother who has been very supportive from the time his interest in writing was discovered at a tender age.

Gladwell’s mother,  Joyce is said to be a Psychotherapist who was born and breadth in Jamaica.

His father, on the other hand, Graham Gladwell happens to be a Maths professor who has lived all his life in England.
