Ron Fisico- Age, Wife, Net Worth, Wedding, Builder, Married, Kids

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Ron Fisico is a Canadian bodybuilder and successful real estate builder who is originally from Ontario. He is widely recognized as the husband of the Trish Stratus. Trish is a Canadian fitness model and professional wrestler who is one of the successful and popular female superstars in WWE.

Trish has proclaimed Diva of the Decade with one WWE Hardcore Championship and three-time WWE Babe of the Year. Having seven years of professional WWE wrestling career, she retired on 17 September 2006. Later in 2013, she was listed in the WWE Hall of Fame.

Married Life With Wife, Trish Stratus

Ron Fisico is happily married to WWE Star Trish Stratus for over a decade. But the most interesting part is the pair is together for over twenty-five years.

Fisico and his now-wife, Stratus, first started dating when they were students at Bayview Secondary School. After graduating, Trish started her WWE career and became a global star. There is no doubt many celebrities would have love to date the WWE star. But Stratus was very loyal to her boyfriend and vice-versa.
