Deadpool and Deadpool 2's Actress, Brianna Hildebrand Is Living A Happy Love Life With Her Girlfrien

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Brianna Hildebrand is an American actress who rocketed to stardom when she starred as Negasonic Teenage Warhead in the marvel films Deadpool (2016) and Deadpool 2 (2018). In addition, she is also famous for her role as Elodie Davis in Netflix's show Trinkets from 2019 to present.

She was born on 14 August 1996 in College Station, Texas, the United States as Brianna Caitlin Hildebrand. She is the daughter of Caleb Hildebrand (father), car salesman, and Veronica Hildebrand (mother). As per her citizenship, she is American and belongs to the white ethnicity. So far, she attended the A & M Consolidated High School which is located in College Station, Texas.

What is Brianna Hildebrand Current Relationship Status?

The young Texan actress Brianna who is openly gay is reportedly an unmarried woman. But she is no more single. At the moment, she is in a beautiful love connection with her long term girlfriend, Jonneke Grisham.

Aged 24, Hildebrand met Grisham on the set of the teen lesbian romance drama First Girl I Loved in 2016. That same year, they became their relationship official. Since then, the two have been living a happy life together for nearly four years.

Brianna who became the first-ever LGBTQ superhero role on Deadpool keeps posting snaps along with her partner Jonneke on her IG wall. On April 23, 2019, she posted their vacation photo from Coachella, California with the caption: "The greatest adventure bud". Now, they live together at their Los Angeles apartment.
