Ignas Brazdeikis Girlfriend Jasmina Gusic: Relationship Timeline

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Ignas Brazdeikis is in a long-term relationship with his girlfriend Jasmina Gusic. It is believed that Ignas and Jasmina have been involved romantically for more than five years.

Despite many speculations from the fans, they aren’t engaged or have been married yet.

Ignas Brazdeikis is a Lithuanian-Canadian professional basketball player. He competes for the Zalgiris Kaunas of the Lithuanian Basketball League and the Euro League.

Brazedeikis attended Holy Trinity CSS High School. Later on, he transferred to Orangeville Prep in Mono, Ontario, Canada.

In 2018, he opted to play for Michigan after a verbal agreement was already on the table between Ignas and the college.

After a prosperous collegiate career, he was drafted 47th overall in the 2019 NBA draft by the Sacramento Kings, shortly being traded to the New York Knicks.

During his professional career, he has played for the New York Knicks, Philadephia 76ers, and Orlando Magic and is now currently on a two-year contract with the Zalgiris Kaunas, starting in 2022.

Likewise, Brazedeikis was a silver-medal winner at the 2015 FIBA Americas U-16 Championship with Team Canada. However, after a certain turn of events, he decided to represent the Lithuanian national team in international competitions.

Ignas Brazdeikis Girlfriend Jasmina Gusic: Relationship Timeline

The shooting guard for Zalgiris Kaunas is in a romantic relationship with his beautiful girlfriend, Jasmina Gusic.

It has been speculated that Ignas and Jasmina have been in a relationship for more than five years.

Ignas and Gusic met each other for the first time in a bar in Oakville. Following this meeting, the two became friends before dating. 

As per reports, it was Brazdeikis who, with sheer determination and full of confidence, convinced Gusic to date him. Eventually, the two started dating in their hometown in Oakville, Canada.

Jasmina prefers maintaining her distance from the paparazzi culture. Despite being the girlfriend of a notable basketball player, the information on Gusic is as close to non-existent.

However, it is known that Jasmina celebrates her birthday every year on the 20th of May. Even though her actual age isn’t known, she is believed to be in her early 20s.

Lithuania FIBA World Cup Journey

Ignas Brazdeikis is a part of Lithuania’s men’s national basketball team competing in the FIBA World Cup 2023.

Lithuania started off the tournament pretty well, defeating Hungary, Egypt, Mexico, Greece, and a shocking win against the USA.

However, Bogdan Bogdanovic led Serbia past Lithuania in the first FIBA World Cup 2023 quarterfinals with a game-high 21 points.

Similarly, Serbia handed Lithuania their first loss in the tournament and eliminated the Baltic nation from the trophy contention.

Ignas Brazdeikis played a pivotal role in helping his home nation reach the FIBA World Cup quarterfinal. The loss against Serbia came as a shock to the Lithuanians, whose eyes were all set on the trophy.

Regardless of this, we remain optimistic that Brazdeikis and his team will come back even stronger in the next competition.
