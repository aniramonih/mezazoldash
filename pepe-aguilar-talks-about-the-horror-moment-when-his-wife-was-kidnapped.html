Pepe Aguilar talks about the horror moment when his wife was kidnapped

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 19, 2024
Updated On December 1, 2023 | Published By: Lisa Arnold | In Child , Married
  • The kidnappers never kidnap someone without a motive.
  • They surely have some reasons behind kidnapping someone.
  • But the kidnapped family suffers the most.

Pepe Aguilar went through the same horrific emotion in his life when his wife got kidnapped. Life became further harder for the family, as his son was behind bars.

Let’s dig deep to know in detail!!!

The life during Pepe’s wife kidnapped

On September 9, 2013, Pepe Aguilar was called for an interview where he revealed the horror moment that occurred in their life when his wife named Aneliz Aguilar Alvarez was kidnapped and held for ransom.

He was totally unknown about the horror moment that is occurring to his wife and when he kept calling his wife, she did not receive and after some time, she called him but her voice was totally changed something as scared which made Pepe doubt it and said:

“It was the type of kidnapping that they’re calling them “express kidnappings.” What they do is they kidnap you and they put you in the trunk of your car just drive you around take your car, take your money, go to the ATM get all the money they have and then after 4-5 hours drop you in the middle of nowhere.”

Son was facing a terrifying time in his life

Pepe Aguilar had already gone through the terrifying time during his wife was kidnapped but Was that not enough? He again went through the greatest trauma when his 24-years-old son named Jose Emiliano Aguilar was arrested for the Smuggling Charges on March 14, 2017.

He was allegedly smuggling four Chinese men into the United States by hiding them in the trunk of his car. According to the insiders and other resources, his son got into many illegal charges for crossing into the United States as he was caught red-handed at the San Ysidro Port of Entry in San Diego.

He was also accompanied by female passengers. The officers of U.S. Custom and Border Protection (CBP) searched his car, Chrysler 200 with a K-9 unit and found four Chinese citizens with non-official documents to enter the United States.

And as a part of the investigation, the car was seized. Those days were very heavy for his whole family when they were facing such a horrible problem of being kidnapped.

And in one of the interviews, Officers opened up that Chinese men are ready to pay the amount between $3000 and $60,000 for the successful smuggling into the United States.

As we recently got the news that Jose was realized after he paid a $15,000 bond and was ordered to live with his mother. But he is now not allowed to leave Southern California and was sent for counseling.

Jose revealed something about the incident:

“We understand the interest in our client Jose Aguilar because of his father’s musical career. However, this case has nothing to do with Pepe Aguilar. Jose Aguilar is young man who will address this matter in court, and not in the press. We understand that Pepe Aguilar will provide a statement shortly. In the meantime, we ask that you respect the family’s privacy.”

Short Bio on Pepe Aguilar

Pepe is a Mexican-American singer-songwriter and actor. He has his own record label Equinoccio Records. He also has a studio with the highest technology in Latin America. Pepe has won four Grammy Awards and four Latin Grammy Awards. He is the son of Mexican singer-actors Antonio Aguilar and Flor Silvestre. More Bio…
