John Graham net worth: John Graham is an American software engineer and reality TV star who has a net worth of $5 million. John Graham is probably most famous for being a contestant on the ABC reality dating series The Bachelorette.  He appeared as a contestant trying to win the heart of bachelorette Becca Kufrin. John Graham made waves on the show in 2018 when he implied that he invented the Venmo payment app. Technically speaking, he HELPED create Venmo. Graham was the fifth employee at Venmo and worked at the company from 2011 to 2016. According to his LinkedIn, he was the company's "Happiness Engineer".

In 2012, Venmo was acquired by Braintree for $26.2 million. In 2013, PayPal acquired Braintree for $800 million. By 2017, Venmo was processing more than $17 billion in payments annually. Today John Graham is a product engineer for Fin Exploration Company. He also describes himself on LinkedIn as an Emoji Consultant.
