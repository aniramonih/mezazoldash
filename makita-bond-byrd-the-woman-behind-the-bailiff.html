Makita Bond-Byrd: The Woman Behind the Bailiff

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Makita Bond-Byrd is a television personality, producer, and the wife of Petri Hawkins-Byrd, the famous bailiff of the Judge Judy show. But who is Makita Bond-Byrd and what is her story? In this article, we will explore her background, career, and personal life.

Early Life and Education

Makita Bond-Byrd was born in the United States of America to unknown parents. She has two sisters, Tiffany and Monalisa Thompson, who are both successful entrepreneurs in their own fields. Tiffany is a designer and the founder of Duett Interiors, while Monalisa is the founder of MB Skin Care, a cosmetics line that sells products like eye correctors and exfoliants.

Makita graduated from the City University of New York – Brooklyn College with a bachelor’s degree in broadcast journalism in 2010. She developed an interest in media and entertainment from a young age and pursued her passion through her education and career.

Career as a Producer

Makita Bond-Byrd has worked as a producer for several CBS shows, such as The Talk, Jeff Probst, Anderson Cooper, and Dr.Oz. She has also worked as a lifestyle expert for KTLA and as a full-time talent coordinator for Telepictures for one year.

Most recently, Makita worked as a production assistant for the Judge Judy show from 2013 until 2020, when the show ended. She was responsible for assisting the production team with various tasks, such as booking guests, conducting research, and coordinating logistics.

Marriage to Petri Hawkins-Byrd

Makita Bond-Byrd is married to Petri Hawkins-Byrd, the longest-serving court show bailiff in television history. Petri is best known for his role as Bailiff Byrd on the Judge Judy show, where he worked alongside Judge Judy Sheindlin for 25 seasons.

Makita and Petri met in 2012 on the Sunset Bronson Studios lot, where the Judge Judy show was filmed. They went on a date at the time, but soon lost touch. They reunited in 2016, when Makita landed a producer role on the same show. They hit it off and got married in 2019.

Makita and Petri have a blended family of six children from their previous marriages. They also have a grandson named Elijah. Makita and Petri are very supportive of each other and share a strong bond of love and respect.

Health and Recovery

In early 2021, Makita Bond-Byrd was diagnosed with a brain tumor and had to undergo surgery in April 2021. The operation was performed by Dr. Black and was a huge success. They were able to remove 95% of Makita’s tumor and she recovered well.

Makita shared her journey of diagnosis, treatment, and recovery on social media and received an outpouring of love and support from her fans and friends. She also thanked her husband Petri for being by her side throughout the ordeal and for being her rock.

Bonding with Byrd

Makita Bond-Byrd and Petri Hawkins-Byrd host their own talk show-like series called “Bonding with Byrd” on their YouTube channel and Facebook page. The show features the couple discussing various topics, such as relationships, family, health, entertainment, and more. They also invite guests, such as celebrities, experts, and friends, to join them on their show.

The show aims to provide laughter, inspiration, and information to their viewers and to showcase the bond that Makita and Petri share. The show has been running since October 2018 and releases a new episode every Tuesday.

Conclusion

Makita Bond-Byrd is more than just the wife of the bailiff. She is a talented and accomplished woman who has made a name for herself in the television industry. She is also a loving wife, mother, grandmother, sister, and friend who values her family and relationships. She is a survivor who has overcome a major health challenge and has come out stronger. She is a star who shines bright with her personality and charisma. She is Makita Bond-Byrd, and this is her story.
