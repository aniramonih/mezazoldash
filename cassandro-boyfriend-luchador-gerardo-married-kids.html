Cassandro Boyfriend: Luchador Gerardo

In the tumultuous world of professional wrestling, Cassandro, known in real life as Saúl Armendáriz, had a significant chapter in his personal life that played out both behind the scenes and in the spotlight.

His relationship with Gerardo, a pivotal figure in his life, offers a glimpse into the complexities of love and fame within the wrestling industry.

Before a high-stakes match against Son of Santo, Saúl and Gerardo found themselves at a crossroads, leading to a temporary breakup.

Saúl grew weary of Gerardo’s desire to keep their relationship hidden, a secret tucked away from the prying eyes of the wrestling world.

Moreover, Gerardo confessed to preferring Saúl when he was just an ordinary luchador, raising concerns about how Saúl’s growing fame was altering the dynamics within the wrestling community.

After a grueling showdown in the ring against Son of Santo, Saúl approached Gerardo with a heartfelt desire to reconcile.

Saúl was even willing to continue keeping their relationship under wraps, but Gerardo, at this juncture, chose a different path.

His wife had discovered their affair and issued an ultimatum: cease all contact with Saúl or risk losing his marriage.

The situation was a painful reminder of the complexities and sacrifices that often accompany love in the wrestling world.

Cassandro Married Life

Cassandro’s personal life, especially his romantic entanglements, has garnered significant attention.

It’s important to clarify that Saúl Armendáriz, the real-life persona behind Cassandro, has not been married.

While the film “Cassandro” portrays his marriage to a partner, Gabriel Vásquez, this was a fictional element introduced for cinematic storytelling purposes.

Saúl Armendáriz, in his own life, has experienced the challenges and joys of romantic relationships, including a long-term relationship with a secret lover who was married to a woman.

This clandestine affair lasted for 12 years and took a toll on Saúl emotionally.

It was a complex and challenging aspect of his personal life that he openly discussed, reflecting the real-life struggles and complexities that individuals within the LGBTQ+ community often face when navigating their relationships.

Cassandro Kids
