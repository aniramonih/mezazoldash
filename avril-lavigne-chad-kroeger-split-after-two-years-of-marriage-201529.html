Avril Lavigne, Chad Kroeger Split After Two Years of Marriage

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Things really are so complicated. Avril Lavigne announced on Wednesday, Sept. 2, that she and her husband, Chad Kroeger, are separating after two years of marriage.

The "Complicated" singer took to Instagram to share the sad news with fans. "It is with heavy heartse that Chad and I announce our separation today," Lavigne wrote, alongside a smiling snapshot with her estranged husband. "Through not only the marriage, but the music as well, we've created many unforgettable moments. We are still, and forever will be, the best of friends, and will always care deeply for each other. To all our family, friends and fans, thank you sincerely for the support."

Lavigne, 30, and the Nickelback frontman, 40, tied the knot in July 2013. Us Weekly was first to report that Lavigne and Kroeger were headed for a split last September.

"It's over," a source told Us at the time. "He has been going around L.A. telling people that they are divorcing."
