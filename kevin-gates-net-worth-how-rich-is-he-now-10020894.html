Kevin Gates Biography

Kevin Gates is an American rapper, singer, and entrepreneur who was born on February 5, 1986, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. His real name is Kevin Jerome Gilyard. Gates was raised by his grandmother in Baton Rouge after his mother and father split up. At the age of 13, Gates started rapping and recording his music. He attended Baton Rouge Community College, where he studied psychology, but dropped out to focus on his music career. In 2007, Gates released his first mixtape, "Pick of Da Litter."

Gates gained popularity in 2013 with his mixtape "The Luca Brasi Story." The mixtape was well-received and led to him signing a deal with Atlantic Records. His debut studio album, "Islah," was released in 2016 and peaked at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart. The album was named after his first daughter, Islah. In addition to his music career, Gates is also an entrepreneur. He has his own record label, Bread Winners' Association, which he founded in 2010. He has also launched a successful line of energy drinks called IDGT (short for "I Don't Get Tired").
