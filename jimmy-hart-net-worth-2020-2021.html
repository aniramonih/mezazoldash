Career

He started his career of being a wrestling manager by managing Jerry “The King” Lawler, who was his high-school friend. But they soon part ways and Hart even formed a group which included King Kong Bundy, “Ravishing” Rick Rude, “Leaping” Lanny Poffo, Jim “The Anvil” Neidhart, Ox Baker, Kamala, “Macho Man” Randy Savage, “Hot Stuff” Eddie Gilbert, The Iron Sheik, and Kevin Sullivan to attack Lawler. He had a feud with Lawler and Kaufman for over a year. Austin Idol, Masao Ito, and Gilbert reached NWA/AWA International titles around 1984 with the help of Jimmy.

He appeared in many shows such as PMG Clash of Legends in 2007, Florida Championship Wrestling in 2008, WrestleMania Axxess, and many more. He also promoted an all-female wrestling show Wrestilicious which premiered on MavTv and BiteTv on 1st March 2010. He also appears on the VOC Wrestling Nation radio program on WNJC.

Hart was first seen on Total Nonstop Action Wrestling in October 2003, where he was seen hyping Hulk Hogan and Jeff Jarrett. H e was again seen in TNA during a match between The Nasty Boys and Team 3D. He again returned to WWE on 1st March 2011. He also appeared on SmackDown: Blast from the Past on 10th April 2012.

Awards & Achievements
