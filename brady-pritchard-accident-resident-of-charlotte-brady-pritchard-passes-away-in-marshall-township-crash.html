Brady Pritchard Accident; Resident Of Charlotte, Brady Pritchard Passes Away In Marshall Township Crash

Monday morning, a collision in Marshall Township resulted in the death of a 22-year-old male.

The Michigan State Police were dispatched to the region of Verona Road, which is located immediately north of H Drive N and west of 13 Mile Road, in order to investigate a collision that occurred at approximately 11 a.m.

It was discovered by the responding troopers that a vehicle had veered off the roadway and slammed into the leaves of the trees.

Mr. Brady Pritchard, who was 22 years old and from Charlotte, passed away at the scene of the accident. The events that lead up to the collision are not known.

.According to the MSP, it is not clear whether the crash was caused by drink, drugs, or rapid driving.
