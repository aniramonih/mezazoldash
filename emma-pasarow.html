Along for the Ride Actress Emma Pasarow Biography

26-years-old Emma Pasarow (born on January 23, 1996) is an actress from Los Angeles who has been gaining immense attention after her great landing as Auden in the Netflix film “Along for the Ride” on May 6, 2022, in which Director Sofia Alvarez featured in the character of a girl whose whole life was about school, that’s why she didn’t get a lot of time for anything else, but Auden decided to change that in the summer before college and one day; she met to meet Eli (Belmont Cameli).

In 2022, on the one side, Emma came into the spotlight for her fabulous performance in “Along for the Ride,” but her fans will put her again in the headlines when they watch her starring in “Kickstart My Heart,” “Waubay,” & “Return to Sender.”

 

Emma Pasarow Birthday & Family

The 5 feet 6 inches tall charming actress Emma Pasarow (age 26 years) took birth in Los Angeles at her father Ted Pasarow and mother Susan Pasarow’s house on January 23, 1996. As a kid, she and her sister Anabel Pasarow watched many movies, which ultimately impacted Emma’s mind and motivated her to become an actress.

Her schooling got completed in 2014 at Harvard Westlake, Los Angeles, CA, after which she joined Wesleyan University. From WU, the lady graduated in 2018 with a Bachelor of Arts in the theater.

While at her time at Wesleyan, she participated in numerous stage shows like “Ugly Lies the Bone,” “Frank,” “Far Away,” and “The Fear Project.” But for the first time, she had the taste of acting while studying at Harvard Westlake when her drama teacher offered her play to the lead role in “The Matchmaker.”

 

Who is Emma Pasarow’s Boyfriend?

Famous American actor and CEO of Anonymus Content LLP Steven Aaron Golin’s son Ari Golin is the one Pasarow has been dating for a few years. Emma met Ari while studying at Harvard Westlake, and after a few meets, they started dating each other.

Pasarow’s boyfriend is also an actor, just like her. He has been in productions like “Pam & Tommy” and “Superkid.” They both are at the beginning of their careers, and with each other’s support, Emma and Ari are progressing each year.

 

Career

Just like any other struggling actor at the beginning, it was tough for Pasarow to find a role in any production. She appeared for a dozen auditions, but things were not working out. Finally, the girl connected with a talent managing agency called Clear Talent Group.

In 2019 she became part of a short movie called “Lena,” a Gosha Shapiro-directed movie about a nine-year-old Russian girl trying to pursue education at an American school. The next project that Emma got was “Forget-Me-Not,” again a low-budget film in which her role was of a girl named Grace.

Pasarow was having a tough time pursuing her career because of a lack of quality work, then one day, her agent Adam Liebross called and told her to audition for a movie called “Am I Ok?”. Luckily she cleared the auditions, and the moviemakers offered her to play the role of Courtney in the film.

At the beginning of April 2022, Emma appeared as Penny in Showtime series called “Super Pumped,” a biographical movie about the founder of a famous taxi service called Uber. This show took Pasarow’s career to heights, and definitely, her appearance in “Along for the Ride” would also help her land more significant roles in the future.
