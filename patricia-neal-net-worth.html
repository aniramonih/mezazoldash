FAQs about Patricia Neal

Who was Patricia Neal?

She was born on January 20, 1926, in Packard, Kentucky, USA.

What are some of Patricia’s most famous roles?

Some of her most notable roles include Helen Benson in The Day the Earth Stood Still (1951), Marcia Jeffries in A Face in the Crowd (1957), Emily Eustace Failenson in Breakfast at Tiffany’s (1961), and Alma Brown in Hud (1963).

Did Patricia win any major awards?

Yes, she won several prestigious awards, including an Academy Award for Best Actress, a Golden Globe Award, a Tony Award, and two BAFTA Awards.

Where did Patricia study acting?
