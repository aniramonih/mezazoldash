Will there be '1000-lb Sisters' Season 5? Here's everything you need to know

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 8, 2024

DIXON, KENTUCKY: Now that '1000-lb Sisters' Season 4 has ended, fans are wondering whether there will be another season of TLC's reality show. Fans have always been curious to know what's happening in the Slaton sisters' life. Indeed, there is a possibility for the show to get renewed soon.

A lot of things happened in Season 4 of the show. Tammy met Caleb Willingham, the love of her life, at an Ohio rehab center. After knowing each other for just 3 weeks, the couple got engaged and soon started planning their wedding. In the finale episode of '1000-lb Sisters' Season 4, we saw Tammy's wedding day. Well, there is still more to the story. Fans are curious to know what happens after marriage. Will Tammy and Caleb have their happily ever after? Keep reading to know everything about the future of the show.

When will '1000-lb Sisters' Season 5 release?

There has not been any official announcement about whether '1000-lb Sisters' has been renewed for Season 5 or not. Since the show has just ended, the streaming platform will take some time to confirm the future season. The network tends to wait a few weeks after the finale episode to announce a new season. Indeed, there is plenty of hope for the future. If the new season is to happen, expect '1000-lb Sisters' Season 5 to release somewhere in 2024.

What will '1000-lb Sisters' Season 5 be all about?

If the fifth season is to happen, we can expect the makers to show what happens after Tammy and Caleb's marriage. It has been previously reported that the couple has been facing friction in their marriage. Since Caleb is still in the rehab center and Tammy has not got a new house, both are maintaining the long-distance marriage. That obviously becomes difficult to handle at times. Therefore, we can expect the new season to feature the ups and downs of the couple's marriage.

On the other hand, if the new season is to happen, it will give us a clearer view of Amy and Michael's divorce rumors. It is expected that the new season will bring up both Amy and Michael's perspectives toward their marriage and whether they want to officially continue it or not. If not, then who will get custody of their children, and how the divorce will affect their mental state? A lot of things need to be answered and only a new season can do so.

Who will feature in '1000-lb Sisters' Season 5?

The show is about the Slaton sisters, therefore, we can not expect anyone else other than Tammy and Amy Slaton to be in the lead role. Moreover, the show will undoubtedly feature Caleb, Michael, and other family members including Amy's children, her parents, and other siblings.
