About the Documentary

The documentary film “The Disappearance of Shere Hite” directed by Nicole Newnham was released in 2023. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and offers insight into the life of Shere Hite, a renowned sex educator and feminist who lived in Germany. The film, narrated by Dakota Johnson, garnered positive reception following its premiere at Sundance on January 20, 2023, and subsequent release in the United States on November 17, 2023.

Produced by a team including Nicole Newnham, Kimberley Ferdinando, Trevor Smith, Molly O’Brien, R. J. Cutler, and Elise Pearlstein, the documentary showcases Shere Hite’s influential contributions to sex education and feminism. With a focus on accessibility, the narration by Dakota Johnson in simple English enhances the storytelling experience.

The documentary, distributed by IFC Films and Sapan Studios, aims to shed light on the experiences and impact of Shere Hite, offering audiences valuable perspectives on her life and work.
