Hamilton cast Daveed Diggs is dating his girlfriend Emmy Raver-Lapman.

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Tony Award-winning actor, Daveed Diggs is on the road to success after facing failure in originality. It seems like the Blindspotting star has now found the key to success to achieve his goals while focusing on clearing out all the obstacles while pursuing the field of entertainment.

California-born actor, Diggs loves to rap, which is a type of music that consoles all the rhythm of our body and soul. So, is the acting sensation dating someone or single as of 2020? Find more about Diggs' personal life in the following sections.

Who Is Daveed Diggs Girlfriend?

The 39-years-old Daveed is in a blissful romantic relationship with Emmy Raver-Lampman. Daveed's beloved girlfriend Emmy is an American actress and singer, who is well-known for her appearance as Allison Hargreeves in the TV series The Umbrella Academy.

The couple had their first meeting upon the set of Hamilton, a music play in 2015. But, Daveed was dating at the time so that Emmy became a close friend to him while portraying the broadway musical, Hamilton.

Fortunately, the friendship only remained close for two or three years after the duo started dating in 2019. The pair then spotted together for the first time on the Film Independent Spirit Awards on 23rd February 2019 with their beloved friend, Janina Gavankar.

The loving couple has kept their dating behind their highlighted professional lives, and occasionally appears together on the media's attention. Currently, Emmy is starring on the Netflix superhero web series, The Umbrella Academy, since 2019.

Once Lived in a Joyful Relationship with Jane the Virgin Actress!

Before starting a relationship with Emmy, Daveed once dated an American actress, Jalene Goodwin, who also served as a casting director for The Three Canvases back in 2015. The duo lived in Washington Heights, Manhattan, with their pet dog, Soccer.

The former couple's relationship was running quite better for more than a year, but after that, they didn't stay in their relationship due to some reason. They then separated amicably in 2016.
