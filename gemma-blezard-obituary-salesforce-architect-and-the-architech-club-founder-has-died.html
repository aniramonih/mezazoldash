Gemma Blezard Obituary, Salesforce Architect And The Architech Club Founder Has Died

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 17, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Gemma Blezard Obituary – With great sadness, we must share the news of the passing of Gemma Blezard, founder of The Architech Club and active member of the Salesforce community. Gemma’s extraordinary journey, unyielding outlook, and powerful leadership made her a household name. Her premature passing had far-reaching effects beyond the technology industry. As a Salesforce Architect who had been certified 19 times, Gemma was no stranger to success and innovation.

Every project she worked on demonstrated her passion and enthusiasm, which garnered her recognition and respect from the business. She launched The Architech Club in the fall of 2019, and the company’s comprehensive Salesforce consultancy has flourished under her leadership ever since. In addition to her business successes, Gemma had a truly remarkable personal story. Gemma was quite open about her battle with cancer on her blog, which she titled “Gemma’s Little Corner of the World.”

She overcame serious health problems and continued her job in the field with remarkable resilience and strength. Gemma also launched Ladies Be Architects, an initiative to encourage and empower women to pursue careers in computer science. She motivated many others in her quest to increase diversity and inclusion in the workplace. All of us in the Salesforce family and beyond are devastated by her passing. As much as we mourn her passing, we rejoice in the life of a woman who refused to be defined by her adversities.

Gemma Blezard left behind an inspiring story of resilience and optimism that will continue to inspire others for years to come. There are no adequate words to express the depth of our sorrow at your loss, which we share with your loved ones at this time of mourning. We are deeply saddened by the news of the passing of such a promising individual.
