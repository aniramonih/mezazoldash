Glynn Washington Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Glynn Washington is the host and executive producer of Snap Judgment, a Public Radio Exchange (PRX) and NPR radio show. Washington was born in Detroit, Michigan in 1970 and later moved to a farm in rural Michigan with his family. He received a bachelor's degree in political science and Asian studies from the University of Michigan in 1992 and in 1996 got his JD from the University of Michigan's law school. He is married to Anne Campbell Washington and has two children.Glynn Washington won the Public Radio Talent Quest sponsored by Public Radio Exchange (PRX) and the Corporation for Public Broadcasting (CPB), which had more than 1,400 entries. Shortly after winning he developed the Snap Judgment radio program, which first aired in July 2010.From 2007 until 2010 Washington was the Director of Young Entrepreneurs at Haas (YEAH), a program at UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
