Lil Mabu | Family And Ethnicity

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Lil Mabu, also known as Matthew Peter DeLuca, is a young rapper from New York who gained notoriety in 2022 with the release of his single “No Snitching.”

In 2019, DeLuca made his professional debut. Similarly, he became well-known after his song “Miss Me” was released in 2020.

In addition, he collaborated with Dusty Locane on the song “No Snitching,” which became popular on TikTok.

He dropped Double M’s, his debut mixtape, on June 30, 2022.

In addition, Mabu has collaborated with several musicians, such as Dougie B, Howiee OO, Ej Banks, ZayBinSteppin, DD Osama, Kay Flock, and DThang.

Lil Mabu is well-known for his style, which combines elements of hip-hop and trap music with a carefree and positive vibe. Fans appreciate the rapper’s songs as a result.

Was Lil Mabu banished?

Renowned rapper Lil Mabu recently received his diploma from Collegiate School, a premium school located in Manhattan. But a lot of doubts have been raised over his dismissal.

The rapper appears to have been expelled multiple times before graduating.

Similarly, Lil Mabu has made numerous videos available on his YouTube channel disclosing that his songs were the reason for his expulsion.

The rapper made fun of his teachers in a number of his songs. Thus, this may have been the cause of Mabu’s dismissal.

In addition, he writes songs about education; in fact, a few of them were recorded in classrooms.

As of right now, none of the reliable media outlets have released any information about the rapper’s expulsion.

But a lot of TikTok users and YouTubers have posted the video of Lil being kicked out for criticizing his professors.

It became clear what the subject was when the up-and-coming rapper posted a picture of himself graduating on June 10 with the caption, “THEY AIN’T WANT ME TO GRADUATE, BUT I MADE IT HAPPEN!!”

In addition, Lil Mabu posted a video of himself celebrating his graduation, and his admirers showered the comment section with well wishes.
