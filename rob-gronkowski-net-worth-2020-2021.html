Early Life

Robert Gronkowski was born on May 14, 1989, in Amherst, New York, to his parents Gordon Gronkowski and Diane Walters. He grew up in Williamsville, New York.

He went to Williamsville North High School and used to play football and basketball. He used to play for the athletic team Spartans. During his juniors, Gronkowski made 73 tackles, seven touchdowns, and 36 receptions.

Rob Gronkowski is in a relationship with cheerleader Camille Kostek since 2015. All his brothers have played football for their colleges.
