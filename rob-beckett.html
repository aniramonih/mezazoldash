Career

Beckett started his career in the year 2009 by doing stand-up comedy and also participated in So You Think You’re Funny and secured the third position. This how boosted his career and motivated him to do more. He was also invited to the Adelaide Fringe Festival, held in Australia.

After his performance at the festival, he was nominated for the best newcomer award in the year 2011. In the same year, he made his debut on television with a guest appearance in the series Fresh Meat as Mike, the show was telecasted on channel 4. Later, in the year 2012, Rob officially made his debut at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival with his individual show “Rob Beckett’s Summer Holiday”.

He got many big projects and one of them was co-hosting I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! NOW! along with Laura Whitmore and Joe Swash. The entertainer gained huge fame from this but in 2015, he left the show. He appeared in various shows like Rock’N’Roll, The Magic Sponge, Celebs Go Dating, 8 Out of 10 Cats, More4, etc.

On July 7, 2016, he joined YouTube and began posting his videos on it. Rob is officially verified from YouTube and has over 40.3k subscribers.
