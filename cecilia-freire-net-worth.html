Cecilia Freire Wiki Biography

Cecilia Freire was born on 17 November 1981, in Madrid, Spain, and is an actress, best known for appearances in various productions such as “Physical or Chemical”, “Velvet” and “No Shame”. She’s been active in the industry since 2000, also being known for her television work. All of her endeavors have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Cecilia Freire? As of early-2018, sources estimate a net worth that is at $400,000, mostly earned through a successful career in acting. She’s also acted in stage projects, and has gained several awards over the course of her career. As she continues working, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

As a teenager, Cecilia already found her way into various acting roles in both Spanish television and film. She studied acting at a young age, and aspired to become a professional in the film industry. She began appearing in various short films, and had support roles in series which started to increase her popularity. Soon, more opportunities would come her way which would increase her net worth; she found herself in a starring role in the teen series “Physical or Chemical”, and was cast in more mainstream productions as the years passed.

Freire became a part of the romantic comedy “Shamelessly”, which was her first major role, and followed it up with appearances on television in “La Vita de Rita” and the short film entitled “La Habitacion de los Abriogs” which was released in 2005. She then became a part of several Telecinco series, including “Central Hospital” and “Tirando a Dar”, and continued her way through Spanish television with her breakthrough in “Mortaledo y Filemon” in 2008, which would gain her a lot of acclaim. Her net worth increased even further as more projects started to come in; she returned to the series “Physical or Chemical” before gaining a supporting role in the film “8 Dates”, then in 2010 Freire was cast as the lead role in the series “Impares Premium” playing the role of Lola, followed by appearances in numerous short films, such as “Anque todo vaya mal” and “Bitch”, all contributing to her net worth.

In 2013 she starred in “Solsticio” and then “Dragons High School”, before joining the series “Velvet” in which she played Rita Montesinos, the best friend of character Ana, appearing with other popular Spanish actors such as Miguel Angel Silvestre, Natalia Milan, Diego Martin and Manuela Velles. While being a part of the show, she also starred in “La Sonrisa de las Mariposas” IN which she played Teodora Galiana, continuing to raise her net worth.

Aside from her film and television work, Cecilia has also appeared in theatre productions, including being a part of “La Katarsis del tomatazo” and “The Winter’s Tale”, and gaining a lot of acclaim for her theatrical performances.
