Kris Jenner Boyfriend Corey Gamble Is The Target Of Kanye West Accusation

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Kanye West criticizes his former collaborator, Corey Gamble, who is Kris Jenner’s long-term lover. “God has a plan to remove the godless Corey needed to never be here anyway,” the “DONDA” artist, 44, shared on Saturday afternoon.

However, in a now-deleted earlier post, the rapper made the huge declaration that the matriarch’s lover “cheated” on her with a lady at a nightclub. 

Kanye further written in his scathing Instagram post, “And I think he’s a nice person Not a great person A nice person who used to be around Puff’s family then got around Justin Bieber and then when Kris got divorced he slid in He became the tv version of a father figure and as he always called hisself ‘a REAL n***a’ He once told my wife he knew what music she should be listening to So when I seen him a week later I had him removed from my daughters birthday party,” the singer claimed.

According to some sources, Kanye hasn’t approved with Kris, as he referred to her as “Kris Jong-un” on Twitter in the summer of 2020 and blamed her of “white supremacy” during one of his now-famous outbreaks. 

Interestingly, in his latest post, Kanye honored Kris and wrote, “I love Kris This woman is a hero and she’s done what she’s had to do to protect her family and make sure they prosper even if it meant telling everyone not to listen to me,” West wrote. 

Fast forward to 2022, and the hitmaker is back on the limelight, but this time he has his sights set on his ex-Kim’s lover Pete Davidson.

Maybe getting along with his mother is his most recent approach for reuniting with his estranged wife, Kim Kardashian, since he recently opposed several of her divorce appeals and continues to criticize her current partner, Pete Davidson.

How Long are Kris Jenner and Corey Gamble in a Relationship?

Kris and Corey began dating in 2014, only one year after her divorce, and they continued to show off their relationship on social media.

Corey met Kris at designer Riccardo Tisci’s 40th birthday party in Ibiza and started publicly dating the matriarch of the Kardashian family in November 2014, after her divorce from Caitlyn Jenner.

The on-off couple has split several times in their nearly seven-year romance, and they broke in March 2017 after Kris told Corey “she was getting sidetracked with this relationship and that she needed to put her family before her own needs and wants.”

However, the duo appears to be going strong as Corey celebrated Kris’ birthday on his Instagram page and said, “Happpppppy Birthday my Beautiful Queen!! Love you Deep bab. Cheers To the best mother & more decades of Love and good health with u.“

Corey celebrated his 41st birthday on November 10, and Kris took her turn to wish Corey a happy birthday on Instagram.

“Happy birthday to my love @coreygamble!!

“You are the most amazing partner, best friend, confidant, travel aficionado, therapist, problem solver, golf buddy, protector, advisor, and the best, most wonderful man to my kids and my grandkids!!!

“You are such a special part of my heart and soul and I love you so very much babe… thank you for all you are in my life!! I am truly blessed.”

Corey is the same age as 65-year-old Kris’s daughter Kim, making him 25 years Kris’ junior.

Kris Jenner Boyfriend Corey Gamble Used To Be Justin Bieber’s Manager

Corey Gamble is a business executive and skilled manager. He is currently working for the entertainment and marketing company SB Projects of the famous record producer Scooter Braun. Presently, his role is that of a tour manager at the company.

The 41-year-old was born on November 10, 1980, and grew up in Atlanta. Before attending Morehouse College, he studied at Westlake High School to learn business marketing. Following his graduation, Gamble began working for Scooter Braun and became the manager of pop star Justin Bieber.

Gamble worked as a tour manager at SB Projects and shared a close bond with Bieber, even referring to the singer as his “nephew” at one point.

 The manager gained further popularity on social media after his relationship with Kris Jenner came to light.

Net Worth of Kris Jenner Boyfriend Corey Gamble

Corey Gamble has a current net worth of about $3million.

Corey currently resides in Beverly Hills and is was regularly featured on Keeping Up With The Kardashians, which aired its last show on June 10, 2020.
