Grace Helbig net worth and salary: Grace Helbig is an American comedian, actress, author, talk show host, and internet personality who has a net worth of $6 million. Grace Helbig was born in Gloucester County, New Jersey in September 1985. She is the creator and host for the YouTube channel it'sGrace. Helbig is also the creator and host of the podcast Not too Deep with Grace Helbig and the comedic talk show The Grace Helbig Show on the E! television network. She created the web series DailyGrace on My Damn Channel from 2008 to 2013.
