Who is Hannibal Buress?

Tall and handsome Hannibal Buress is an American renowned stand-up comedian, actor, writer as well as producer.

He is best known as the Stand-up comedian who was a writer on Saturday Night Live and 30 Rock. As an actor, he is known for appearing as Griffin in the comedy movie Daddy’s Home.

What is the birth name of Hannibal Buress? Bio, Age, Parents

Hannibal Buress was born in Chicago, Illinois on 4 February 1983. He is of African American ethnicity and has an American nationality.

He is the son of Margaret(mother) and John Burress(father). His birth name is Hannibal Amir Buress.

His father worked for Union Pacific Railroad and his mother was a teacher. He grew up with his elder sister.
