Erin O'Connor Married, Husband, Children, Boyfriend, Affairs &amp; Net Worth

Swan-like, porcelain-skinned, hard-working, tall with a height of 6 ft, beautiful, wealthy, whatever-Erin O'Connor is just one of those models that never gets older or out of fashion. Her looks are so distinctive and elegant that she looks like a Modigliani in real life. 

Who is Erin O'Connor?

Erin O'Connor is a famous British-Irish fashion model. She was born on 9 February 1978, in Brownhills, West Midlands, England. She grew up in a Catholic family where her father is from Ballycastle, County Antrim, Northern Ireland.

There are no details about her siblings and her early life on the internet. For education, she attended Brownhills Community School. On a school trip to the Birmingham Clothes Show, she was spotted by a talent scout. 

Juergen Teller released her first photos for issue 159. For John Galliano, Christian Dior, Donna Karan, Prada, Versace, Miu Miu, Jasper Conran, Giorgio Armani, Julien Macdonald, Jean-Paul Gaultier, Chado Ralph Rucci, Badgley Mischka, and Dolce & Gabbana, she has modeled on the catwalk.

She didn't just lay her hand on modeling but also in radio and television. In 2001 she appeared as herself in Absolutely Fabulous's "Paris" episode. Likewise, O'Connor wrote and presented for BBC Radio 4, two fashion documentaries, Erin's Model World and In Search of Beauty. 

She is still active in the modeling industry with great enthusiasm. O'Connor is also a very good friend of popular comedy actress, producer, presenter, and businesswoman Cleo Rocos and journalist kate Silverton.
