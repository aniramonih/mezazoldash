Itzan Escamilla is a Spanish actor known for playing the role of Samuel Garcia Dominguez in Netflix's international hit series, Elite. The show features an ensemble cast including Maria Pedraza, Miguel Herran, Jaime Lorente, Mina El Hammani, etc. Further, Itzan has appeared in tv shows like The Ministry of Time, Victor Ros, and Cable Girls.

Movies, TV Shows, and Theater

In 2016, Escamilla made his television debut on the historical detective series Víctor Ros. The same year, he appeared in two different shows, Seis Hermanas and Centro Médico.
