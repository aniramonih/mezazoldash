Jack Osbourne wants to be a real-life cop! But what will Ozzy say?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

What will Ozzy Say? Now son Jack wants to be a real-life cop!

By Caroline Graham for The Mail on Sunday
Updated: 09:40 EST, 17 July 2011

Jack Osbourne seen in the reality show Armed And Famous

His father is known as a wild man of rock, but Jack Osbourne has revealed he wants to become a  full-time police officer.

Reformed alcoholic Jack, 25 – son of former Black  Sabbath frontman Ozzy – trained as a reserve police officer for reality TV show Armed And Famous in 2006.

Now he plans to turn his back on Hollywood to pursue his dream.
A source said: ‘Jack has been doing bits and pieces, but he says he is fed up with Hollywood. He loves being a reserve police officer and wants to go into law enforcement full-time.

‘He says his work on the streets is far more  satisfying and fulfilling than anything he has ever done in showbusiness. He wants to be a regular officer on the street catching the bad guys.’

Osbourne trained as a reserve police officer in small-town Muncie, Indiana. He has returned regularly to work with the Muncie force and last month tweeted ‘This was a lot of fun!’ above a report about a drugs bust he was involved in.

On July 1, he tweeted ‘Another day at the office’ above a picture of himself in his Muncie police  uniform. Muncie police sergeant Jay Turner confirmed Osbourne returned regularly to patrol the streets, adding: ‘He doesn’t make a big to-do about it. He just wants to be a regular cop.’

Jack and Ozzy last year donated a £5,000 police dog called Ozzy to the Muncie force.

Armed And Famous follows stars as they train to become police officers and who patrol in Muncie, Indiana

The source added: ‘Jack is talking about training in LA to join law enforcement here. He wants to do something that matters with his life.

‘He is back working out regularly because he wants to get in great shape before he enters the police academy.’

Osbourne has been open about his struggles with drugs and alcohol,  saying he got drunk for the first time at 13 and started taking drugs at 14.

Jack took on the Sahara desert when he starred in the television programme in ITV2 programme, 'Jack Osbourne: Adrenaline Junkie 2' in 2006

He went into rehab and credits AA with keeping him clean and sober.

He has previously spoken of his childhood dream of joining the police force, saying: ‘That was what I always planned to do before I got involved in the show [MTV’s The Osbournes] and started hanging out in Hollywood.’

Ozzy Osbourne, famous for biting the head off a dove in a meeting with record label executives, has had numerous brushes with the law as a result of his own addictions.

He was once arrested for assaulting his wife Sharon and was jailed for  public intoxication in 1994 and 2004. In 1982, he was arrested and charged with disorderly conduct for urinating on the Alamo memorial in Texas.

Jack has appeared in several Hollywood films and TV shows including Austin Powers: Goldmember, and starred in his own 2003 TV series, Union Jack, on Channel 4.

He lost four stone for the 2005 show Adrenaline Junkie, which aired on ITV2 and featured him taking part in high-octane sports such as rock-climbing and martial arts.

Jack Osbourne’s spokeswoman refused to comment.
