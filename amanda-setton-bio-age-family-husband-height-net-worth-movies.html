Amanda Setton Biography

Amanda Setton is an American actress best known for her recurring role as Penelope Shafai on the CW’s teen drama Gossip Girl. Also, she is known for playing Kimberley Andrews on the ABC soap opera One Life to Live and General Hospital as Brook Lynn Quartermaine.

Additionally, she made her Off-Broadway debut in the comedy play Love, Loss, and What I Wore and was on the first half of season one of the Fox comedy The Mindy Project. She has played small roles in the 2008 films Sex and the City and What Happens in Vegas.

Moreover, she appeared as Shauna Dicanio in the series The Mindy Project and it was later reported that her character would be written out of the show. In February 2010, the website Daytime Confidential said that Setton had taped her last episode on One Life to Live as Kim despite being offered a contract and also a pay increase.

Amanda attended a high school where she was the vice president of the theater club. Later, she attended Ithaca College where she majored in drama and graduated in 2007. Later, she studied during her junior year in Barcelona, Spain in an IES program. She later completed a comprehensive two-year Meisner technique program under the instruction of Eliza VanCort at The Actor’s Workshop of Ithaca.
