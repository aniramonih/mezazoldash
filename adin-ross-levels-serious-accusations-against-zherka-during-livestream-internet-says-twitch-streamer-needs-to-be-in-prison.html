Adin Ross levels serious accusations against Zherka during livestream, Internet says Twitch streamer

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 9, 2024

MIAMI, FLORIDA: Adin Ross has once more garnered attention for engaging in a conversation with Twitch streamer Zherka. During their live stream discussion, Ross directly questioned Zherka about a sensitive topic, asking if he was involved in "pedophile."

Zherka's frank responses sparked a substantial online buzz, resulting in criticism from a number of his followers. Furthermore, Ross confronted him regarding the alleged involvement in such conduct. These contentious conversations have continued to enhance Ross' reputation as a controversial streamer.

In a recent Adin Ross livestream, he had a Discord conversation with Zherka, during which he posed a direct question to Zherka, asking if he had been accused of pedophilia, saying, "Sneako said you are a pedophile?"

It's worth mentioning that this discussion came about after Sneako informed Ross about certain allegations against Zherka, prompting Sneako to end the stream with Ross. Subsequently, Ross reached out to Zherka to seek clarification.

Later, Adin Ross confirmed that he had indeed accused Zherka of being a pedophile and mentioned that Sneako had initiated the entire conversation. Zherka responded by admitting to the accusation but asserted that he was unaware of Ross' prior knowledge regarding this matter.

Zherka added, “What the f**k, is Sneako just talking s**t?” Further, the conversation continued, and Adin Ross proceeded to inquire whether Zherka did not perceive his actions as peculiar. 

However, Zherka mentioned visiting a state where such behavior was entirely lawful. Nevertheless, Ross appeared utterly bewildered and raised further questions.“Don’t you think it is weird to f**k 16-year-olds?”

Zherka acknowledged by admitting that it was undeniably unusual but explained that they were attracted to each other, so he visited a state where such actions were legal and engaged in the activity.  

Adin Ross reacted by saying, "Yeah, Nah, come on bro."
