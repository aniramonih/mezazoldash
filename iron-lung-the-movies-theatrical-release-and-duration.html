Iron Lung: The Movies Theatrical Release and Duration

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 5, 2024

About Iron Lung Movie

Get ready for the release of “Iron Lung,” a new and thrilling science fiction film directed by Mark Fischbach, also known as Markiplier. The movie is based on the video game “Iron Lung” by David Szymanski and marks Markiplier’s debut as a full-length movie director. Filmed in Austin, Texas, the movie promises a truly terrifying experience with an abundance of blood, even surpassing “Evil Dead.”

Iron Lung’s Theatrical Release

Yes, “Iron Lung” is intended for theatrical release, marking a significant milestone in Mark Fischbach’s career as a filmmaker. Despite some production delays caused by a strike, the movie is expected to hit the big screens. The specific release date is yet to be announced.

Exploring the Plot

The film’s narrative unfolds in a dystopian future following an apocalyptic event known as “The Quiet Rapture.” The storyline revolves around an intriguing mission set in an eerie ocean of blood on a desolate moon, undertaken by a courageous prisoner aboard the “Iron Lung” submarine. As the convict navigates through this hazardous environment, they encounter various dangers and daunting circumstances.

Developing Iron Lung

The production of “Iron Lung” involved significant effort from Mark Fischbach and his team. Despite challenges and delays, including a strike, the movie is on track for its theatrical release. The official trailer for the movie was released on Markiplier’s YouTube channel in October 2023.

Theatrical Duration

While the specific duration of “Iron Lung’s” theatrical run is not specified, the movie has garnered significant anticipation with its intense trailer, hinting at a dark and mysterious storyline. Directed, produced, written, and starring Mark Fischbach, the film promises to be one of the bloodiest horror movies, attracting eager audiences to witness its exploration of an ocean of human blood on a desolate moon.
