Kim Hye-yoon Biography

Kim Hye-yoon is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on November 10, 1996 in Seoul, South Korea.

It was after she finished college (graduation ceremony in February 2019), that Kim began auditioning for bigger roles, including for JTBC’s satire drama Sky Castle. The massive success of the series along with her distinctive role as an ambitious and unfriendly but ladylike high school girl, launched her to stardom and won her the 55th Baeksang Arts Awards for Best New Actress.

In 2019, Kim starred as the protagonist for the first time with MBC’s school fantasy Extraordinary You, based on the hit Daum webtoon July Found by Chance, in which she won both New and Excellence Actress at the MBC Drama Awards.
