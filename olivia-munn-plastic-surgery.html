Fillers in the cheeks: Olivia Munn’s cheeks look different in recent pictures in terms of their height and shape. Jawline Contouring: Olivia Munn’s jawline looks rounder and less sharp than it did before, which suggests that jawline contouring might be possible. Dermal fillers are used in this process to change the shape or size of the jawline. Radiesse and other gel-like fillers are often used to add volume and make the chin stand out more.
