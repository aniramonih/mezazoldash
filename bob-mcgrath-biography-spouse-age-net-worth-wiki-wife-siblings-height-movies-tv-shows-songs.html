Bob McGrath Biography: Spouse, Age, Net Worth, Wiki, Wife, Siblings, Height, Movies &amp; TV Shows, Song

Robert Emmett McGrath, famously known as Bob McGrath, is a multi-talented American actor and singer known for his television, film, and theater work. He has also been a voice actor, author, and musician.

Some of his most well-known roles include playing Bob Johnson on the popular children’s show Sesame Street and providing the voice of Bob the Builder in the animated series of the same name.

He has also released multiple albums and written several books, showcasing his talents and versatility as a performer.

Early Life And Education

Bob McGrath was born in Ottawa, Illinois on June 13, 1932. He was the son of Edmund Thomas McGrath, a farmer, and Flora Agnes (Halligan) McGrath.

McGrath attended the University of Michigan and received a Bachelor of Arts degree in music. He then studied music at the Manhattan School of Music and the Juilliard School.

Career

McGrath’s passion for music started early. He studied at prestigious institutions like the University of Michigan and the Manhattan School of Music.

He also served in the US Army, using his musical talents to entertain troops and bring joy to their lives.

In the 1960s, he gained national recognition as a featured tenor on “Sing Along with Mitch,” a popular television series that showcased his incredible vocal abilities.

Through his talents and dedication to his craft, McGrath became a beloved figure in the music industry, inspiring countless individuals to pursue their passions and share their gifts with the world.

However, in 2016, McGrath announced his retirement from the show, marking the end of an era. His impact and legacy on “Sesame Street” will never be forgotten despite his departure.

His dedication to educating and inspiring children through music and his kind and gentle demeanor will continue to live on, making him a cherished part of the show’s history.

Personal Life

Bob McGrath was married to Ann Logan Sperry, his college sweetheart, for over 50 years until she died in 2018.

They had four children: Liam, Robert Lily McGrath, and Alison McGrath Osder. Bob was a devoted father and husband who often incorporated his family into his work, including having his son appear on Sesame Street with him.

McGrath died from complications of a stroke at home on December 4, 2022, at age 90.
