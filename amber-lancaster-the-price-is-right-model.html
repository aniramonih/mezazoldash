Who is The Price is Right model Amber Lancaster?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

FOR OVER 10 years, fans of The Price is Right have seen Amber Lancaster grace their screens.

Joined by host Drew Carey, Amber often showcases the prizes and items that are being given away on the show.

Who is model Amber Lancaster?

Amber Lancaster is a model and actress from Tacoma, Washington.

Her career in modeling dates back to her teenage years, when she was crowned Miss Washington Teen USA.

After winning the title, she spent five years as a member of the Seattle Seahawks' cheerleading squad before moving to Los Angeles, California, to pursue a career in entertainment.

When she arrived in Hollywood, Amber quickly joined the cast of The Price is Right and landed guest roles on popular shows like Days of Our Lives,  CSI: Miami, The Bold and the Beautiful, and Entourage.

On the modeling side, she has appeared in features for Maxim, US Weekly, People Stylewatch, Life & Style, and Star, according to her biography.

When did Amber Lancaster join The Price is Right?

Amber has been a member of The Price is Right family since 2008.

In March 2023, she celebrated her 15th anniversary on the show with a touching tribute in honor of the show moving from Bob Barker Studio 33 to a new set at Haven Studios in Glendale, California.

"I’ve spent the last 15 years of my life on stage 33. I’ve been picked up, twirled around, cried on and danced with," Amber said.

"I’ve adopted two dogs (shoutout to @friendsofeastvalley), announced my pregnancy and made friends who have become like family. I’ve seen people get engaged, beat cancer, win a trip for their 50th wedding anniversary, win a car when they’ve been riding the bus, win $10k when they’ve just lost their job.

"The fact that I get to be even a small part of those experiences is what makes me the most grateful," she continued.

"I get to be part of a legacy, a part of our culture, a show we watched with our grandparents and show our kids...a good ‘ol slice of American Pie, and most of all a little sliver of happiness to brighten someone’s day... stage 33 has hosted the likes of everyone from Elvis to Johnny Carson. And although @therealpriceisright isn’t going anywhere, it’s still the end of an era and a little piece of my heart will always be at Television City. See you in Glendale."

Season 51 marked to final one taped at the studio, which was named after the show's original host, Bob Barker.

Is Amber Lancaster married?

When Amber isn't on set, she can often be found at home, spending time with her son, Russ.

She welcomed Russ during her previous marriage to businessman A.J. Allodi, which ended in 2020.

Amber gave birth to her son in 2019, but the delivery was not as smooth as an expecting mother would hope.

In August 2022, she opened up about her traumatic birthing experience, revealing that she ended up on a ventilator in the ICU and on dialysis treatments.

She began: "POV: you’ve just survived severe preeclampsia/ HELLP and delivered your baby prematurely at 31 weeks. You hemorrhaged during the emergency c-section and had to have a partial hysterectomy which lead to acute kidney failure.

"You’re transferred to the ICU for CRRT dialysis but there is a major complication and the catheter is mistakenly placed in your carotid artery instead of your jugular vein. You go into hemorrhagic shock, acute hypoxic respiratory failure, and acute pulmonary edema amongst other things, while suffering another massive hemorrhage. You wake up on a ventilator in the ICU.

"Your family is told there is a chance of brain damage but they won’t know until you’ve woken up. Finally, 3 surgeries, 2 massive transfusions, 2 days on a ventilator, 6 liters of blood loss, 20 something pounds of fluid removed and 5 days later, your angel of a nurse orchestrates a visit between dialysis treatments (it’s the 24 hour life support kind so not much time in between)…. And you finally get to hold your baby for the first time.

"Out of all of those treatments, I believe this is the one that saved my life," Amber continued.

"This is my yearly reminder that life is fragile, precious, and not guaranteed. Life definitely isn’t fair, but if I’ve learned anything, It’s that it’s not about the hand you’re dealt, it’s about how you play your cards."

Not much is known about Amber's previous marriage, but her social media pages indicate that she has since moved on and is now dating a man by the name of Matt.
