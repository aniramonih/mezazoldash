Hovig releases acoustic version of &quot;Gravity&quot;

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 30, 2024

Eurovision Song Contest 2017 might have ended but the participants have just started their musical journey. Many artists like Manel Navarro and Isaiah have released their singles over last few months. Some of the tracks including “Streets of Gold” & “Keep On Falling” have received great reactions from listeners. Hovig follows suit.

Hovig’s Contest Single – Gravity

The list now includes Cyprus’ participant Hovig Demirjian. The artist represented his country in Eurovision Song Contest 2017 with the single “Gravity”.

This song has an Imagine Dragons’ vibe and is quite different to love songs that we get to hear nowadays. Its energetic beats resonate lover’s wish to surpass everything to be with his/her beloved.

An Upbeat Acoustic Track

Well, Hovig has given us yet another beautiful rendition of his track. Today, the singer released an acoustic version of “Gravity” that is as good as the original. It has helped him showcase his vocal prowess and his control over his sound. Give it a listen below:
