Drake Gets OVO Owl Braided Into His Hair

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Drake has made waves with his latest hairstyle as he somehow had his signature OVO owl designed as part of his braids.

A hairstylist by the name of Bri Marie hooked the 6 God up with her expertise for the owl design on Tuesday (February 21), which included some jewelry with a pair of gold rings to make what would be the eyes of the animal pop out.

AD LOADING...

“I already hear y’all now lol ‘Drake Got an Owl Braided in his head.’ ‘OVO’ It’s only right to make this a reality. I’d be lying if I say I don’t shock myself,” the Miami-based stylist wrote on Instagram.

She added: “TIME AND TIME Again. God thank you, you’ve truly blessed me; but, I know this is your plan!”

Drake has stuck with his braids for the most part since introducing them in 2022, but briefly took them out last August while on vacation in Turks and Caicos. He debuted a greasy slicked-back look that felt as if he was put in a time machine going back several decades.

In an Instagram post at the time featuring him gambling with the hilarious vintage hairstyle, he sought out nicknames for the persona from his IG followers.

AD LOADING...

“Wolf of 6 street,” Kaash Paige wrote, while The Game suggested: “Lionel Hella Richie.”

Quavo kept things in the wrestling world with an ode to Scott Hall, who passed away in March. “RAZOR AUBREY RAMONE,” he said.

Justin Bieber chimed in with: “Clarance Donovan aka steezeman deluxe.”
