Brief Bio of Kyle Larson, Age

The famous American professional stockcar racer Kyle Miyata Larson was born on July 31, 1992, in Sacramento, California. His parents are Mike and Janet Larson, who have two other children besides Kyle. The aspiring car racer saw his first motor racing experience a week after his birth but joined the gang at the age of seven. According to the records, Kyle received basic training while practicing his sport. He is considered a caring guy who loves the water and hopes to go on a cruise with his family.

Kyle started out with outlaw karts in Northern California, and today he currently drives the Chevrolet SS No. 42 for Chip anassi Racing and competes full-time in the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series. Although he has a limited schedule with the Xfinity Series, where he drives a number 42 Chevrolet Camaro for CGR, the car racer is also involved in the Camping World Truck Series with the number 24 Chevrolet Silverado as a race car.
