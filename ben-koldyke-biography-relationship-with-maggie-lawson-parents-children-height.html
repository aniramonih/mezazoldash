Short bio

The Hollywood actor/director was born Benjamin Koldyke on March 27, 1968 in Chicago, Illinois in the United States. He grew up near Kenilworth and after graduating from high school attended a private college institute called Choate Rosemary Hall. He also studied English at Dartmouth College in New Hampshire, graduating in 1991.

While in high school, he was a quarterback for his school’s football team. He also taught English in high school, after which he became a football coach in Chicago. Koldyke jumped from the field to the stage after meeting Rob McElhenney, a well-known American screenwriter/producer/actor/director.
