Overall Meaning

The song "No. 9" by YoungBoy Never Broke Again is a love song that expresses the artist's love and devotion to his partner. In the first verse, the artist sings about his lover's beauty and how he loves to see her in her Bond No. 9 fragrance. He also talks about his wealth and how it doesn't matter to him, but he wants his partner to know that he's capable of taking care of her. The artist promises to love his partner and make her happy, while also emphasizing that their partnership can lead to great accomplishments. In the second verse, the artist expresses his desire to take his partner out and have fun with her despite his busy schedule. He also talks about his love for his partner, referring to her as his "lil 38 baby" and promising to never hurt her.


Throughout the song, the artist emphasizes his devotion and loyalty to his partner. He promises to make her happy and protect her, while also boasting about his wealth and success. The lyrics are straightforward and easy to understand, with a simple yet catchy melody that complements the message of the song.
