Is Cardi B left handed? Who is the concertgoer she threw the mic at in the viral video?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Cardi B recently made headlines when an incident at Drai’s Beach Club in Las Vegas went viral. While she was performing, an audience member threw a drink at her, and in response, the rapper threw her mic back at the fan. The video captured the moment, showing Cardi B wiping off the drink from her face and fixing her hair after the altercation. Her security personnel immediately rushed to the front of the stage to handle the situation.

Cardi B throws microphone at audience member who threw a drink at her. pic.twitter.com/alLgHMFshb

— Pop Base (@PopBase) July 30, 2023

Interestingly, this wasn’t the first time Cardi B reacted this way during a performance at Drai’s Beach Club. The night before the drink-throwing incident, she appeared to be frustrated with the DJ cutting off her songs too early and threw her mic at the DJ on that occasion as well.

As the videos spread on social media, many netizens were surprised and questioned if Cardi B is left-handed, as she held and threw the mic with her left hand. Celeb Answers confirmed that the rapper is indeed left-handed and even writes using her left hand, which is less common among the population.

Responding to the incident, Cardi B shared a video where she said, “WATER AND GAS INCLUDED…Literally #Jealousy,” possibly implying that jealousy might have been the motive behind the drink-throwing act.

WATER AND GAS INCLUDED …Literally #Jealousy

While many fans and internet users showed their support for Cardi B, others criticized her for reacting in an erratic manner. Instances of artists throwing mics toward fans have occurred in the past, sparking debates about how artists should be treated during performances.

As of now, the identity of the fan who threw the drink at Cardi B remains undisclosed, and the reason behind the act is yet to be revealed. It is said to be a woman who committed the act, but no further details have been disclosed about her.
