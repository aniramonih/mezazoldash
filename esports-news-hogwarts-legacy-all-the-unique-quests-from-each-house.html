Hogwarts Legacy: All the Unique Quests From Each House

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 29, 2024

The latest open-world RPG sensation of the gaming world, Hogwarts Legacy, has been deemed one of the greatest games of 2023, even though it was released very early in the year. Warner Bros’ new venture in the gaming world with Hogwarts Legacy has opened the gates for a lot more gaming adaptations of their IPs. 

The Hogwarts Legacy is directly based on the Harry Potter franchise. It offers an in-depth interactive experience in the fascinating Wizarding World. The main attraction of the game, the Hogwarts school, offers source accurate spectacles and experiences, such as Ghosts, moving paintings, and Student Houses. 

Unique quests of each House in Hogwarts Legacy

Each house in Hogwarts Legacy offers different common rooms, uniforms, and quests. Here we talk about the different quests of each house. 

Ravenclaw (Ollivander’s Heirloom)

The weakest House Quest in the game. In this, players have to talk to Gerbold in Olivander’s shop in Hogsmeade. He will assign them to find his lost wand. This will lead them to the Owlery and talk to Richard Jackdaw’s ghost and solve a puzzle to retrieve the wand. 

Unique location: Owlery 

Gryffindor (The Hunt for the Missing Pages)

One of the ‘spookiest’ unique house quests. In this, players have to retrieve lost pages of a mysterious book they found. For that, they need to talk to the ghost of Nearly Headless Nick, who will tell them to bring him rotten meat from the kitchen. If they successfully collect the meat, Nick will take them to the graveyard. 

Unique location: Access to the kitchen while being a Hufflepuff student

Hufflepuff (Prisoner of Love)

The most interesting unique quest in the game. Here, players travel to Azkaban prison with Auror Helen Thistlewood in order to visit the prisoner Anne. She was the former love interest of Richard Jackdaw who was convicted of his murder. This quest reveals the terrible truth of the whole story of Jackdaw.

Unique Location: Azkaban prison

Slytherin House (Scrope’s Last Hope)

It is one of the most thrilling quests in the game. Players have to help a house elf named Scrope to find him an heirloom that he wants to gift to the headmaster. In this quest, players are led to a cave where they have to fight monsters and enemies to find this item. 

Unique location: Caves
