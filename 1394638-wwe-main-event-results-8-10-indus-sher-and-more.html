WWE Main Event Results (8/10): Indus Sher And More

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

WWE presented the newest episode of WWE Main Event on August 10. The show aired on Hulu.

The matches were taped ahead of WWE RAW at the Target Center in Minneapolis, Minnesota, on August 7.

The results, courtesy of Cagematch, are as follows:

  • Indus Sher (Veer & Sanga) (with Jinder Mahal) def. Apollo Crews & Akira Tozawa
  • Nikki Cross def. Tegan Nox
