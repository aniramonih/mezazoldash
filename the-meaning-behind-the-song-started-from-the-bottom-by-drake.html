The Meaning Behind The Song: Started From The Bottom by Drake

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

The song “Started From The Bottom” by Drake was a massive hit when it was released in 2013. The track became an instant hit and a popular anthem for those who are grinding and hustling to achieve their goals. The song is a powerful reminder of the value of hard work and perseverance, even when it seems like the odds are stacked against you. In this article, we’ll explore the meaning behind the song and why it struck such a chord with listeners.

  • The Inspiration Behind The Song

The Inspiration Behind The Song

Drake’s life story is one of the things that makes him such a relatable and inspiring figure for so many people. He grew up in a rough neighborhood in Toronto, Canada, where he faced many challenges and setbacks. His parents divorced when he was young, and he struggled with dyslexia, which made academic studies difficult for him. Despite these obstacles, he always had a passion for music and used it as an escape from the stresses of life.

As he got older, Drake started to make a name for himself in the Toronto music scene. He released a few mixtapes that gained him some recognition and eventually caught the attention of Lil Wayne, who signed Drake to his record label, Young Money Entertainment. From there, Drake’s career took off, and he became one of the biggest names in hip-hop.

However, despite his success, Drake has never forgotten where he came from. He often speaks about his upbringing and credits it with shaping him into the person he is today. It was this reflection on his past that inspired him to write “Started From The Bottom.”

What The Song Is About

“Started From The Bottom” is a song about Drake’s journey to success. In the track, he raps about his humble beginnings and the struggles he faced on his way to the top. The chorus of the song sings “Started from the bottom now we’re here,” which has become a popular catchphrase in pop culture.

The song is a testament to the idea that hard work and perseverance can pay off, even when things seem difficult. Drake raps about working multiple jobs, having to wear hand-me-down clothes, and the feeling of being overlooked and undervalued. But despite the obstacles in his way, he kept pushing forward and ultimately achieved his goals.

The song’s lyrics speak to anyone who has ever felt like their dreams were out of reach. It’s a reminder that success is possible if you’re willing to put in the work and stay focused on your goals.

Why The Song Was So Popular

“Started From The Bottom” was a massive hit when it was released, topping the charts and quickly becoming a fan favorite. There are a few reasons for this.

First, the song has a catchy beat and a memorable chorus. It’s the kind of song that you can’t help but sing along to, and it’s perfect for blasting in the car or at a party.

Second, the song’s message is universally relatable. Everyone has dreams and aspirations, and everyone has faced obstacles in their lives. “Started From The Bottom” is a song about overcoming those obstacles and achieving success, no matter how difficult the journey may be.

Finally, Drake has a way of connecting with his fans that few other artists can match. He speaks candidly about his struggles and his insecurities, and his vulnerability has resonated with fans around the world. “Started From The Bottom” is a perfect example of this honesty and vulnerability, and it’s one of the reasons why Drake has become such a beloved figure in the music industry.

The Legacy Of “Started From The Bottom”

“Started From The Bottom” has become one of Drake’s most iconic songs, and it’s a track that will likely be remembered for years to come. The song has inspired countless people to keep pushing forward in the face of adversity, and it’s become an anthem for those who are hustling and grinding to achieve their goals.

Beyond its impact on individual listeners, “Started From The Bottom” has also had a significant impact on the music industry as a whole. The song was a commercial success, topping the charts in multiple countries and helping to further establish Drake as one of the biggest names in hip-hop. It’s also influenced countless other artists, who have taken inspiration from its message of hard work and perseverance.

In conclusion, “Started From The Bottom” is a song that speaks to the power of hard work and resilience. It’s a reminder that success is possible, even in the face of adversity. Drake’s journey from a rough neighborhood in Toronto to one of the biggest names in music is truly inspiring, and his music has touched the lives of millions of people around the world. “Started From The Bottom” is a perfect example of the impact that music can have on our lives, and it’s a song that will continue to inspire and motivate listeners for years to come.
