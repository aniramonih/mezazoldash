Meet Sawyer Jones - Child Actor From &quot;Antlers&quot; Movie

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

Sawyer Jones was born in 2015 in LA, United States of America. He works as an actor. His nickname is Sawyer J. Jones. He is also a passionate storyteller. 

In 2018, He has appeared in Modern Family and Chicago Med(2020) and made his feature film debut in Antlers(2021), directed by Scott Cooper and produced by Guillermo del Toro.

Family Information Of Sawyer J. Jones

Sawyer Jones is a talented American kid actor. He was born and raised in Los Angeles by his parents. Being an actor he has kept his family information undisclosed.

He has one sibling, a younger brother named  Tiernan Jones. Tiernan is also an actor. He has played a role in Just roll with it(2019), The bridge(2019), and Diary of a Future president(2020).
