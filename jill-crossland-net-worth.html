Jill Crossland, born in Yorkshire, England, embarked on her musical journey at Chetham’s School of Music, followed by the Royal Northern College of Music in Manchester.

Her rigorous classical training set the foundation for her profound understanding and performance of Baroque and Classical music.

She is widely recognized for her preference for the Baroque and Classical periods, with a special affinity for the works of Johann Sebastian Bach.

Career Highlights
