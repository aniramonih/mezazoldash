What Time Does Green Acres Mall Open and Close?

Green Acres Mall opens at 10 am every day and closes at 9:30 pm from Monday through Saturday. On Sundays, the mall opens at 11 am and closes at 7 pm. These opening and closing times are standard hours that apply throughout the year. However, there are certain holiday exceptions; we’ll discuss those below.

Is Green Acres Mall Valley Stream NY Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, Green Acres Mall is open on Saturdays and Sundays. On Saturdays, the mall opens at 10 am and closes at 9:30 pm. On Sundays, the mall opens at 11 am and closes at 7 pm. These hours are the same for all stores in the mall, but individual store hours may differ and dependent on their lease agreement.
